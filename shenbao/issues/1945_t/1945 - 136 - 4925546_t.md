### Detailed Record of the Disaster of the Yuyao Road Fire; The Devastating Situation Urgently Requires Relief from All Sectors - Jiāxī

At around 4:00 PM on the 11th, a major fire broke out in the first slum area of Yuyao Road (Old Singapore Road). It was gradually extinguished at 9:00 AM the next morning. It burned down more than 3,000 huts, more than 10 factories, and two alleys, killing four people and seriously injuring seven. Two firefighters were seriously injured. The scope of the disaster area and the number of victims are unprecedented in recent years.
