現在來看一看傘兵攻勢所用的方法，也是一件有興味的事。在對蘇戰事中，母須應用這全部技術，因爲沒有海洋的阻隔，不必變更飛機戰車攻勢，但是德國果眞企圖侵人英倫的話，就得應用傘兵攻勢，並有兩種重要的輔冀：一是沿海峽長距離射程大砲的掩護砲火，一是海面上海陸軍的聯合進攻。

下面就是標準化傘兵攻擊的全部過程，尙係初次公佈於世，但在克里特戰役中巳極度施展過了。全程可以淸楚劃分做十二個步驟，通常並規定開戰後一小時內須佔據重要機塲至少一處，否則全部戰事都趕不上預算了。

一·最高統帥選定進攻目標，空軍參謀部即對此作詳盡硏究，並擬定進攻計劃。

二·長時間作縝密的空中偵察，以決定明確的最初目標，及附近最適宜有用的降落地點。

三·從高空轟炸設防區域及陣地，如港口和飛機塲。

四·以司吐加急降轟炸機進玫。

五·殲滅機掃蕩敵方空軍；殲滅機是一種長距離的戰鬥機或驅逐機。

六·降落傘部隊開始由空中進攻，最初僅用手槍和手溜彈，在克里特戰役中，士兵都在胸前備帶第二頂應急的降落傘。

七·載傘兵的飛機，兜了一個圈子後，仍飛臨戰塲上空，事實上，只要能够維持機隊的整齊交通，這圈子兜得愈速愈妙，但同時當然還要竭力閃避敵方的高射砲火。在第二次飛過陣地的時候，機內人員拋下了傘兵的別種裝備，主要的是每人一支步槍並一二挺機關槍。機關槍係一種特別式樣，用輕的錳合金製成，分三部份拋下，可以迅捷的合起來施用，步槍都巳實彈，裝在一隻有襯墊的長金屬盒內，並附有一偭包着橡皮的軸輪，以便在地上推動，滾到需用的地方去。有些東西繋在一種三連降落傘上拋下，這傘的各部分自然要比傘兵所用的獨頂傘小得多。各種不同的東西就以傘的顏色來辨別；偽裝的塗色法僅在士兵的傘上小規模的試騐過。

Now let's take a look at the methods used in paratrooper offensives, which is also interesting. In the war against the Soviet Union, there was no need to apply all of these techniques, because there was no ocean barrier, and it was not necessary to change the plane-tank offensive. However, if Germany really intended to invade England, it would have to apply a paratrooper offensive, with two important auxiliary aids: first, the covering fire of long-range coastal artillery across the English Channel, and second, a joint land and sea invasion across the sea.

Below is the entire process of a standardized paratrooper attack, which is being published for the first time in the world, but was already extensively implemented in the Battle of Crete. The entire process can be clearly divided into twelve steps, and it is usually stipulated that at least one important airfield must be occupied within one hour of the start of the battle, otherwise the entire operation will not be on schedule.

1. The supreme commander selects the target of the attack, and the air force staff conducts a detailed study of it and formulates a plan of attack.

2. Conduct lengthy and meticulous aerial reconnaissance to determine the specific initial target and the most suitable and useful landing sites nearby.

3. Bomb fortified areas and positions from high altitude, such as ports and airfields.

4. Attack with Stuka dive bombers.

5. Fighter planes sweep and eliminate the enemy air force; fighter planes are long-range fighters or destroyers.

6. Paratroop units begin the aerial attack, initially using only pistols and hand grenades. In the Battle of Crete, soldiers carried a second emergency parachute on their chests.

7. The planes carrying the paratroopers make a circle and fly over the battlefield again. In fact, the faster the circle is made, the better, as long as the formation of the air fleet can be maintained, but at the same time, efforts must be made to avoid enemy anti-aircraft fire. The machine gun is a special type, made of light manganese alloy, and is thrown down in three parts, which can be quickly assembled for use. The rifles are all loaded and packed in a padded long metal box with a rubber-covered axle wheel to push them on the ground to where they are needed. Shandong Five-Tone Opera 47

— Ze Fu

Five-tone opera is a local opera of Shandong. It is named because its opera includes five tones: Shuanglianhuan (Double Linked Rings), Jiulianhuan (Nine Linked Rings), Wawaqiang (Baby Voice), Dou Hanchong (Fighting Cold Insects), and Ji Shuan (Chicken Tether). It is also known as Five-Person Opera, or said to be due to the five roles of Sheng (male lead), Dan (female lead), Jing (painted face male), Mo (elderly male), and Chou (clown); or it is said that Shandong people do not distinguish between the two words "ren" (person) and "yin" (tone), so ren is actually a mishearing of yin. Locals also call it Zhouguzi, which seems to be a transliteration of the two words "Yangge" (rural folk dance). According to legend, the opera originated in the rural areas between Zhangqiu and Licheng in Qing Dynasty and evolved from Shandong Yangge. Gradually, it flowed into the cities, and its traces could be found from royal families and government officials to scholars and commoners. The scripts are mostly stories of talented scholars and beautiful women, and there are very few great deeds of emperors and generals. Such as Fan Jiangguan, Zhu Yingtai, Wang Erjie's Husband Yearning, etc., all can cater to the psychology of the public, so Shandong people have the title of "stake for tethering a wife," saying that it is welcomed by women, just like the stake that tethers the door. In fact, it is not only women who think so. In addition to the main opera, there is also fen opera, which is more obscene, such as "Peach Blossom Nunnery", which has now been gradually improved. There is no accompaniment of stringed instruments when singing, only gongs, cymbals, drums and clappers are used. At the end of the song, there is usually a long drawn-out coda, and the gongs and drums must also [missing text] to connect with it. The opera is divided into two routes: the eastern route belongs to Licheng, focusing on singing; the western route belongs to Zhangqiu, focusing on performance.

A Suggestion Regarding "Power Restriction" 48

In order to reduce the consumption of remaining fuel, the French Power Company implemented a power restriction measure starting on December 10th of last year. Although the Shanghai Power Company also announced a power restriction and reduction of electricity consumption on the 23rd of the same month with the approval of the Public Works Bureau, users with monthly electricity consumption below 500 degrees are still allowed to use 500 degrees as the limit.

The power restriction measures in the French Concession are more stringent. Its content is that from this day on, each user's electricity consumption should be reduced to 80% of the "average reference electricity consumption." The so-called "average reference electricity consumption" is the average of the electricity consumption of the four months with the highest electricity consumption in the past twelve months. If it exceeds 80% of this average, it will be subject to a penalty of 25 times the fee. According to the current price of 6.2 cents per unit, then exceeding one unit will require payment of 15.5 yuan in penalty.

In order to avoid being penalized, we are also trying our best to reduce electricity consumption, but I think that the records of electricity consumption in the past twelve months are often incomplete for general users, so the calculation of the average will definitely feel very difficult. Therefore, the author advocates that the French Electric Authority should send a special letter to notify each user of the possible electricity consumption limit, and at the same time use red letters to specifically mark it on the monthly bill, so that each user knows what 80% of the "average reference electricity consumption" is. In this way, the power restriction measures can be successfully implemented, and general users can also avoid unnecessary penalties.
