不要茍安

近來社會上普遍流行着一種苟安的心理，處此嚴重的時局之下，這種心理實在是要不得的。

苟安的第一個結果是退縮，不求上進。第二個結果是自私，但是顧自己，不顧大衆。第三個結果是享樂，但圖目前，不計將來。苟安的心理表現在行為上，便是「得過且過」「各人自掃門前雪」一今朝有酒今朝醉」，這種行為使一個人墮落，使一個國家民族不能够復興，在戰爭時期，更是一件莫大危險的事。

在人生奮鬥的大道上，不上進就是後退，後退就是失敗。在敵我鬥爭的塲合中，我的後退，可以促成敵人前進的機會。有時，一些些的後退，可以使你永久不得前進，使你失敗得永久不得翻身。現在是世界整個轉變時期，中國國民是中國圈子內的一份子，中國國家又是大東亞圈內的一份子，任何國民都要「戰戰兢兢，如臨深淵，如履薄冰，」一步一步，謹愼小心，向前進行。這時候不容你靜止，不容你安閒，更不能些微疏忽。所謂「一失足成千古恨，再回頭已百年身。」眞是危急存亡之秋，那裏可以退縮，那裹可以「得過且過」

現在戰爭是全體性戰爭，在全體性戰爭裹，沒有前方後方的區別，沒有軍人平民的區別。任何地方，任何人都要受到戰爭的影饗，就是要想避免，也是沒有法子的。只有大家奮起，努力奮鬥，使勝利早日獲得，方才能得安定。這時候，國家的命運握在國民手裏，國民的命運更和國家的命運聯系着，國家有命，你也有命，國家沒命，你休想偷生。假使大家自私，不巷國家出力，結果是「皮之不存，毛將焉附？」現在正是大東亞戰爭決戰的重要時期，大家共同協力，努力邁進的時候，若要保全自己，先得保衛祖國，若要保衛祖國，先得保衛大東亞。那裹可以自私，那裹可以「各人自掃門前雪」。

享樂是每個人所希冀的，亦是每個人所需要的，可是，最要緊的，切不可以目前的享樂，換得將來的吃苦。在此時此地的環境，目前的享樂是否使將來吃苦，這個問題，用不到說明，只要想一想，假使某一國的人民，個個都求享樂，只圖目前，不計將來，那末國家的勝敗存亡，已不必取決於疆塲之上。假使中國國民是這樣的，那末我們更不必談解放，談自由了，這豈是我們所願意的。我們不願亡國，可是因為我們享樂的結果，使國家走上亡國之路，眞是好危險啊！范文正公說過：「先天下之憂而憂，後天下之樂而樂。」退是我們中國的國民精神，這是我們中華民族維繫永存的唯一原因·現在豈是享樂的時候，更那裹可以「今日有酒今日醉」呢！

緬日陸軍部隊再發動攻勢在全戰綫上發揮威力

東京十七日中央社電緬甸日陸軍部隊，十六日在南部緬印國境辛澤瓦盆地，已將英軍第七師殲滅，而摧破蒙特巴頓反攻緬甸之企圖，此次殲滅戰完畢後，日軍即轉對阿拉坎山脈東方加拉頓河谷之西非軍及中部緬印國境琴丘陵地帶之英印軍，再度首先發動攻勢，與印度國民軍密切協力節節進攻。同時，北部緬甸國境富貢方面，日陸軍精銳各部威力，巳深入敵之內部。按緬甸美英渝三國聯軍，依據魁貝克會談决定之基本方針，企圖奪回緬甸，打通中緬公路，自表面觀，彼此之戰爭目的雖相一致，但實際三方之利害與反攻之用意，則完全不同。且其指揮系統不一，蒙特巴頓僅東南亞細亞軍總司令官，徒有虛名，即對所屬之英印軍固與歐慶萊克意見不一致，難以統率，對於駐印度渝軍及滇省渝軍更無絲毫之發令權，又駐印度渝軍係歸美將官史蒂威爾指揮，滇省渝軍則由總司令官衛立煌指揮，極盼美渝之反攻緬甸作戰，因軍令系統分歧，故當日軍實行猛攻後，即行紛紛潰退，此乃屬當然之勢。敵陣營既有此種根本缺陷，美英渝三軍指揮官之感情關係，亦極缺融和。駐重慶史蒂威爾司令部於十五日發表稱：蒙特巴頓與史蒂威爾最近會見時，曾協議史蒂威爾正式就任反軸心軍總司令官問題，同時協議此後對日作戰方針，此種事實尤為對方之感情將瀕破裂，而亟謀修好之象徵。蒙特巴頓因南部緬印國境之英第七師及第五師遭日軍猛攻，其形勢已吿不穩，仍行命令加拉頓河谷之西非軍第八十一師及中部緬印國境琴丘陵地帶之英軍旅團進擊，期挽回南部緬印國境之頹勢。但日軍早已窺破蒙特巴頓之新謀略，當敵軍尙未集合戰力前，即首先攻擊加拉頓河谷及琴丘陵地帶之敵軍，如是英美渝三軍之聯合反攻緬甸計劃已先後失敗，敵軍難於再行蠢動。當日軍此次在中部印緬國境初發動攻勢之極，緬甸方面日陸軍當局會於三月十日發表極具勝利把握之談話，此項談話完全與此次日軍之作戰實情相符合。

鐵道交通的改善

— （馬家璋）

鐵道交通的改善，可分成乘客，和運輸二方而來說。

就乘客而論，第一個要改善的應是旅客過分擁擠的問題。只要這問題能解决，旅客們至少可減去一大半行旅的苦痛。從現狀中，已經很明白地吿訴我們，旅客擁擠必不是眞正繁榮的現象，完全由走單帮的人太冬的原故造成的。當然改善乘車旅客過分擁擠現象的最好方法，就是肅淸單帮客人。這責任應求三方面能同時負起。第一是統制當局能強化統制，第二是鐵道當局能使物資運輸完全流暢，第三是希望旅客們能自己痛切覺悟。

改善售票方法，也可減少旅客擁擠的程度。售栗處可全部設在站外，終日地工作着。這樣旣可給旅客們一個長時期的購票時間，而消滅了旅客購票時擁擠的情形，並且　黑市票　及一切售票的弊　，都可自然地免除。售票處還可根據統計一般旅客至各地的歡目，再配合火車所能載客的最大能力，這樣我們便能很容易地計算出每日旅客至各地的平均最大數額。依着道數額，售票處便能决定每日至各地售果的多寡，而加以限制旅客，使購不着車票的旅客，不在空跑到車站內去擠軋。因此也可減少軍站和車箱內擠軋的情景了。售票處還可第一天預售第二天的車票，並冉縮短車票的有用性只有一天、這樣或者能够避免許多不肖者的操縱。

現今的火車票價，因爲頭等·二等·與三等的比率太高的緣故；一般旅客都望而却步、無力乘坐頭等·二等者居多。因此火車頭等·二等客車的座位，浪費太多。這種浪費在平日不過只是鐵道當局的損失而已，但在今日車輛正感不嗷應用、旅客十分擁擠的時候，這種無謂的浪費，未免太不應該，不合乎情理了。所以我們希鐵道當局，能完全免除了頭等車的各義，就是二等軍的座位，也應當減至最少數。

車决的排列，我們希望能更精密一點。使得調車的時間盡力減少，車行時間務必準確。這樣不但可減少旅客在車箱中擠軋的痛苦，且也能增加客車的效能。此外軋票時間也可盡量提早。對於鐵道從業員應訓練他們規律·嚴正。處處都能給旅客以指導和協助。

旅客不守秩序，常常會使自己痛苦增加。不守秩序，好像是我國人的劣根性。但我們不能就此不改善。所以希望每個旅客都能顧到大家的利益，例如檢查購票，軋票，上車，下車時，都可依着　序，不一定就要死命的濟軋。尤其是在途中上下車時，道通東輛都有兩個車門，我們正可利用它們一個專用着下車，一個專用着上車、旣可避免秩序混亂擠軋，同時旅客們又省時省力。

就運輸而論，使得物資運輸圓滑，流暢，在論理上最理想的辦法，就是增加車輛。不過在今日戰爭狀熊中，軍事第一的條件下，這常常是不能實現的。但如果我們能將物資運輸的機構，管理，計劃、手段，都能健全起來，雖然是最少數的車輛·我們也能使它們發揮最大的能力，表現最大的效果。今略言如下：

運輸列車效能的研究一般人常以爲車輛的載重能力，若能儘量利用，即使速度甚低，也能得到車輛的最大效用，這實在是似是而非的見解。因爲運輸列車效能的表現，既不是載重的大小，也不是速度的高低，而在兩者得到平衡，使能恰到好處。所以研究載重和速度的配合，仍是運輸最重要的事。因爲徒求車輛　重增大，未必即能獲得效能最大的利用。當然主要路綫較短。物資運輸較　之路，運輸列車常須沿途摘　軍輛，裝卸貨物，因此沿途載重不同，而停車時間父較長，所以很難講求載重和速度的配合。但在　綫較長，物資運輸較盛之路，定有多　整車貨物組成列車，行駛較長的里程，沿途不必停留，或者停留甚少者，鐵道當局對於此項車輛，應講求載重與通度的經濟，以期獲　車稱平均時的最大效用。

Do Not Acquiesce

Recently, a psychology of acquiescence has become prevalent in society. Given the severity of the current situation, this psychology is truly undesirable.

The first result of acquiescence is retreat and a lack of striving for progress. The second result is selfishness, caring only for oneself and disregarding the public. The third result is hedonism, focusing only on the present and neglecting the future. The manifestation of acquiescence in behavior is "getting by," "each sweeping only the snow from his own doorstep," and "a drunken spree today." Such behavior degrades an individual and prevents a nation from revitalizing. During wartime, it is an even greater danger.

On the road of life's struggles, not advancing is retreating, and retreating is failing. In the arena of struggle against the enemy, my retreat can create opportunities for the enemy to advance. Sometimes, a small retreat can prevent you from advancing permanently, causing a failure from which you can never recover. Now is a time of global transformation. Improvement of Railway Transportation

— (Ma Jiazhang)

The improvement of railway transportation can be discussed in terms of passengers and transportation.

In terms of passengers, the first problem to be improved should be the excessive crowding of passengers. As long as this problem can be solved, passengers can at least reduce a large part of the suffering of the journey. From the current situation, it is already clear that the crowding of passengers is not a sign of true prosperity, but is entirely caused by too many people traveling on their own. Of course, the best way to improve the excessive crowding of passengers is to eliminate those who travel on their own. This responsibility should be borne by three parties simultaneously. The first is that the control authorities can strengthen control, the second is that the railway authorities can make the transportation of materials completely smooth, and the third is that passengers can have a deep realization themselves.

Improving the ticket sales method can also reduce the degree of passenger crowding. Ticket sales offices can all be set up outside the station and work all day long. This can give passengers a long period of time to purchase tickets, eliminate the crowding of passengers when purchasing tickets, and eliminate black market tickets and all the malpractices of ticket sales. The ticket sales office can also, based on statistics of the general number of passengers to various places, and in conjunction with the maximum capacity of the train, easily calculate the average maximum number of passengers to various places each day. Based on this number, the ticket sales office can decide the number of tickets sold to various places each day and limit the number of passengers, so that passengers who cannot purchase tickets do not run to the station in vain to squeeze. Therefore, it can also reduce the crowding scene in the military station and carriage. The ticket sales office can also pre-sell tickets for the next day on the first day, and shorten the usefulness of the ticket to only one day. This may be able to avoid the manipulation of many unscrupulous people.

The current train fares are too high for first class, second class, and third class. Most passengers are daunted and unable to afford first class or second class. Therefore, too many seats in the first and second class carriages are wasted. This waste is usually only a loss to the railway authorities, but today, when vehicles are in short supply and passengers are very crowded, this unnecessary waste is too inappropriate and unreasonable. Therefore, we hope that the railway authorities can completely eliminate the various meanings of first-class cars, and even the seats in second-class cars should be reduced to a minimum.

We hope that the arrangement of the schedule can be more precise, so that the time for shunting is reduced as much as possible, and the train running time must be accurate. This can not only reduce the pain of passengers squeezing in the carriage, but also increase the efficiency of passenger cars. In addition, the ticket checking time can also be brought forward as much as possible. Railway employees should be trained to be disciplined and upright, and should be able to provide passengers with guidance and assistance everywhere.

Passengers' lack of order often increases their own suffering. Lack of order seems to be an inferiority of our people. But we cannot just not improve on this. Therefore, I hope that every passenger can take into account the interests of everyone. For example, when checking tickets, checking tickets, boarding, and getting off the bus, they can follow the order, and they do not necessarily have to squeeze desperately. Especially when getting on and off the bus on the way, there are two doors in the east and west sides of the bus. We can use them, one exclusively for getting off the bus and one exclusively for getting on the bus. This can not only avoid chaotic and crowded situations, but also save passengers time and effort.

In terms of transportation, making the transportation of materials smooth and unhindered, the most ideal method in theory is to increase the number of vehicles. However, in today's war state, under the condition of military first, this is often impossible to achieve. But if we can improve the organization, management, planning, and means of material transportation, even with the least number of vehicles, we can make them exert their greatest capabilities and show their greatest effects. Now briefly stated as follows:

Research on the efficiency of transportation trains: Generally, people often think that if the carrying capacity of vehicles can be used as much as possible, even if the speed is very low, the maximum efficiency of the vehicles can be obtained. This is actually a specious view. Because the performance of transportation train efficiency is neither the size of the load nor the speed, but a balance between the two, so that it can be just right. Therefore, the study of the combination of load and speed is still the most important thing in transportation. Because merely increasing the load of vehicles does not necessarily result in the greatest use of efficiency. Of course, on shorter main lines and roads with less material transportation, transportation trains often have to pick up and drop off military vehicles along the way and load and unload goods. Therefore, the load is different along the way, and the parking time is longer, so it is difficult to talk about the combination of load and speed. However, on roads with longer lines and more material transportation, there must be many trains composed of whole carloads of goods, traveling longer distances, and not having to stop along the way, or stopping very little. The railway authorities should study the economy of load and speed for these vehicles, in order to obtain the greatest efficiency of the average time of the vehicles.

In order to implement the above suggestions, the author believes that the railway authorities today should study the following points, determine considerable standards, and implement them effectively.

1. Determine the most economical speed and load tonnage of various vehicles on each section of the line.

2. Stipulate the performance standards that various vehicles should have on each section of the line, so as to compare them with actual performance at any time.

3. Study the vehicle grouping method to reduce the stopping time in each section.

4. For vehicles that do not need to stop or stop very little in each section, their travel times should be stipulated in detail and strictly observed, and efforts should be made to avoid the waste of time waiting for other vehicles.

Using empty vehicles to transport: It is common for railway companies to have empty vehicles in transportation. However, this loss is great, especially now that the number of military vehicles is insufficient, this phenomenon can no longer be tolerated. Therefore, railway managers should strive to reduce the mileage of empty vehicles. If there are too many empty vehicles, in addition to improving the vehicle dispatching method, a fundamental remedy should be sought, which is to increase transportation in the direction of empty vehicles.

Decision on the priority of cargo transportation: Although whatever methods of improvement are imagined in cargo transportation today, the shortage of vehicles is a fact. Therefore, the power of vehicles to transport cargo is limited. When using limited power to transport cargo, of course, the nature and needs of various cargoes should be carefully studied. There should be a decision on who should transport first and who should transport later. The standard for this decision is to look at the degree of relationship between each cargo and the lives of the people today.

The rapid loading and unloading of cargo: The result of the rapid loading and unloading of cargo can of course reduce many of the ills caused by delays. The turnover of vehicles is therefore flexible, so it indirectly increases the transportation efficiency of cargo vehicles.
