如何管制物價？

前日於物價對策，有所闡發，有所獻護，茲更進而一談管理與統制物價中，當局，工商界與社會一般人士所應注意者若干要點如次：

首先，我們始終認定經濟問題是政治問題的一部分，政治基礎優良，機構健全，則經濟上的任何措施，自然輕而易舉。反之，就會費力多而成效少，所謂事倍功半，縱有良法美意，每難悉如預期。過去政府當局施行的各種統制，立法之初意，或者無一不佳，而推行結果，則適成流弊淵籔。爲什麽？就是因爲吃了政治不健全的虧。一般人對於政府辦事失效，輒歸咎於機構之不健全，但是，機構何以會不健全？則還應承認政治基礎不良所致。老實說，幾年來，我們老百姓只聽到許多漂亮的口號，動人的標語，政治上許多毛病，還是改革很少，目前許多無庸諱言的病態，不僅由於機構之不健全，大抵政治根本積重難返，已非一紙命令文吿所能盡革今日之頽風。所以想到今後物價實施全面的管制，更望上下協力，務須盡革過去政治上的病根。否則，實爲管制前途莫大的障礙。

因此，我們可以指出，管制物價雖是管物，但是管物必先管人，尤其是要先管管人的人。把管人的人管好，則一般的人可望管好，而後物也可望管好。談到物價問題，常常痛斥擾亂物價的奸商，而實際上所謂奸商，大概都是社會上比較有塲面有辦法的人，他們都是手眼通天。所以，奸商必須裁抑，而官吏經商尤須認眞嚴禁。政府本有公務員不得兼營商業的禁令，這種法令現在必須貫澈。政府應該淸查一下，若干官吏兼營着商業銀行或企業公司的董事長或董事經理，或若干商人作了官（尤其是金融上經濟上及統制工作上的官），對於這種官而商商而官的二重人格的人，應該限令他們辭官或者辭商，專幹一門而不能任其進退自如，左右逢源，政府能做到這一步，再對實際負責物價管制的各級人員加以嚴格的訓練與考查，則社會視聽必能爲之一新，物價的管制上必可收到重大的效果。

其次，關於執行管制物價的機構與人事方面，第一，是執行機構必須統一，因爲機構不統一，不但使工商各業，有莫知所從之苦，而且互相牽制，互相推諉，互相抵觸的現象，一定不能避免，工作效率，自然要受到阻礙了。要全面的管理物價問題，這是不可不深加注意的。第二，領導機關對於工，農，鑛各業，必須處處採用扶助的態度，一紙命令，是不能濟事的。如果輕於處罰，則使從事生產者有無所措手足之感，如何還能求生產之增加？特別如本報前幾天听指出的，中國是以中小生產佔優勢的國家，小生產的特點是散漫的是薄弱的，對症下藥，只有扶助其發展生產，在經濟上刺激其擴大生產的興趣，資助其擴大生產之能力，這就可以從積極方面以增加生產來解决物價問題。第三，辦理管制工作的人，必須人人拿出天良來，一要認眞，二要無例外。中國社會的通病，就是重情不重法，人人以不受法令拘束爲榮，而只期望別人之守法，尤其是有勢力的地方官員，與富豪劣紳，平常都在盡力求通融不守法中過日子，國家法令，每每小能貫澈，此輩人是罪魁禍首。今後必須痛切注意，破壞此種惡習。第四，執行機構方面，上級對於下級，要多盡責任，指導不厭周群，監督愈嚴愈佳，檢討愈勤愈好，下級機構如推行失當，或發生流弊，上級機構，不獨應負失察之責任，並且應負連帶責任。

管制物價，必須是全面的和澈底的，但是決不能只靠法令文吿，只靠當局的努力，必須全國人民一德一心合力以赴，始能奏效。這裏，第一要求「公開」，對上對下，必須澈頭澈尾，毫不隱瞞，毫不掩飾，事事顯露眞相，處處宣佈實况，這樣公開辦理，一可以消除藉秘密以舞弊的因素，二可以因爲把一切管制懲罰悉數向社會公開，漸漸養成社會制裁力量，人人以不守法爲恥。第三，是需要政府髙官大吏出而做人民守法的模範。我國社會傳統，重情不重理，徇情而枉法的「固有道德」，實在不能存在於今日，而必須以髙官大吏開始，一要講理，二要守法，廟堂上有了大義滅親的盛典，社會上就有破除情面的美事，易俗移風，共濟時艱，是今日刻不容緩之舉，不僅於物價問題爲然了。

英國必須放棄印度麼　（二）

看到甘地耽溺於詭辯和自相矛盾，印度人中間很少有被激怒的。他們直覺地從他的一個意義之中猜出一個意　來，上層階級的印度人，幾乎全都像甘地一樣具有兩種人格，一方面，他們是實用主意者，他們的信念有生活中的具體事實作為根據，另一方面，他們又是神秘主義和浪漫主義者。

大多數的印度人承認這個事實，而且替甘地辯解的時候，說他是以兩種語言來說話的。第一種，是過去的言語，有着神秘主義的形而上的傳統術語，印度民衆瞭解這個另一種，是現代的政治現實主義的語言。兩種聲音都是必要的。

心情跟甘地極不相同的尼赫魯，在解釋這個現象的時候，對我說：「你必須把甘地的人格和作為民族領袖的甘地分開來看。」甘地是一個非常實際政治家，當他實行領導的時候，他很會像任何人一樣地委曲求全和討價還價。但他永遠不放棄說述自己的內心，率直的表現他的個性，表示他是世界的良心，表示他是一個道德家。這兩個角色混合起來　達印度　主義，就往往把外界弄糊塗，覺得後者仿佛是不可理解的胡言亂語了。

儘管我們覺得甘地的話有一些似乎很荒唐的，這並不能取銷他的民族領袖的地位。他是人，他是良心，他是多數印度人所信任的，盲目地追隨而服從着的偉大靈魂。雖然他的私人哲學常常給印度的政治思想以狂妄的影響，但他使出看家本領，計劃一個政治行動的時候，就顯得十分靈敏。他動員了國民大會工作委員會的領袖們，特別是尼赫魯，來提出後來被全印大會通過的措辭聰敏的議案，就能證明這一點。

因此，假如把甘地捉起來關進牢監，大部份的印度人一定會覺得精神上和他一起入獄了。毫無疑義，如果像克利浦斯爵士的聲明以及英政府其他人士所指出的那樣，英國無意於重開談判，那麼，英國也的確沒有路走。但英政府似乎並沒有預見到激起的反應　有目前那樣的規模。

要估計甘地，尼赫魯和其他領袖被捕所發生的影響，我們首先必須記得，他們是印度苦痛的象徵，這苦痛是將近兩個世紀的英國統治所累積起來的，是印度人為驅除這統治而鬥爭的二十年歷史所累積起來的。他們　此為口實，堅決要求立即成立自主政府，雖然聯合國家有着慘重的困難，而國民大會是承認同情聯合國家的。在上述的苦痛之外，現在又加上一個苦痛——示威遊行者對抗英政府絞殺不服從運動的企圖而激起的殺人和流血。

「印度的民族主義者像愛爾蘭人一樣，」最近爲了戰爭問題而和甘地決裂的國民大會領袖之一，拉雅哥伯拉查理（Rojago-Palachari）對我說「他們和英國人鬥爭到現在，還不能把他們已經擁有的東西和新的更惡劣的威脅之間，秤一秤輕重。」

初到印度的英美人士聽印度人說他們自己是「奴隸」，稍稍有點兒驚異，因為在許多方面他們比我們現在更來得自由。大體上，他們比半殖民地更自由。印度人享有着範圍很廣的結社自由，出版自由，和集會自由等等。他們有述說意見的絕對自由。特務　員並不威脅到個人的安全。戰爭尚未增加很多的經濟困難，迄今為止還沒有統　日常必須的食糧。

我在加爾各答，德里和孟買的街上，都看到共產黨召集的羣衆大會示威遊行，羣衆高呼革命的口號，痛斥英帝國主義，紅旗活躍地在微風中飄蕩，沒有人干涉。不久以前，共產黨人很多在獄中，但如今共產黨擁護戰爭，它也就成為合法的了。

在華德哈，在甘地和國民大會計劃推翻英國統治的所有集會中，從沒有人來企圖解散會場，甚至會場附近一個警察都沒有。用許多種文字出版的「哈里　報」，明顯地並不經過審查，把反叛的威脅　　了全國，華德哈的國民大會員們都安然無事。

當俺們微詢我的意見的時候，我大胆向一位國民大會會員說：「你們也許正鑄下最大的戰略錯誤，你們已經有了百分之七十五的自由，如今你們忽略了保衛這個百分之七十五的重要性，而集中力量以爭取其餘的百分之二十五。你們可能把兩者都損失掉的。」然而我所說的，很少有人注意到。

我們必須記得的第二點，是戰前和戰時的舊恨新仇，變成了病症，這病症表現在對英國的亟度懷疑上頭，不論多少邱吉爾式的雄辯，都不能祛除這懷疑。現在，印度人痛恨邱吉爾的理由之中，又增加了一條：在大西洋憲章之中，他特地把印度除外，這是對印度的侮辱。

在最後歸於失望的許多事件中，很多印度人現在舉出克里浦斯訪印的事情來，克利浦斯此行的結果，特別使印度自由主義者和親英的民族主義者傷心。親英的民族主義者們很久以來就把這信心寄託在工業身上，希望工黨能夠親善地在印度造成和平的變革，而在工業之中，克利浦斯是位傑出的人物。所以當他戲劇化地來到印度，在兩個星期之內，談判和印度人合作的條件，而這樣的談判是兩年餘以來印度總督一直規避着的，在這樣的時候，克利浦斯到達印度，全國都湧現着希望，之後，發現了他甚至並未能夠立即給印度以自治的地位，他算錯了，以為保證戰後獨立已經儘够了，殊不知日本的成就已經改變了局勢，印度人目前看戰後的局勢，就像轉世投胎以後的事情一樣的不確定了。

— 黃警頑

物價的高漲，使一般人的生活受到極大的威脅，靠薪水維持家庭的人，一日三餐，已感不易，如果再要負擔一筆子女敎育費，那更是難乎其難了。許多有爲的靑年，在經濟困難的壓迫下，不得不輟了學，這實是社會重大的損失。所以救濟靑年失學，實是很急切的問題，社會上不少熱心人士，舉辦了獎學金助學金等，對淸寒子弟予以熱誠的援助，使他們的升學有一個保障，在這裏要介紹的，就是叔蘋公獎學金。

叔蘋公獎學金創始於民國二十七年，至今已舉辦至第六期，主辦人係叔蘋公後裔顧乾麟先生，顧先生秉承先人遺志，對提倡義務敎育，不遺餘力。叔蘋公獎學金所欲資助的，是家境確係淸寒而無力升學的優秀學生，所以對得獎學生的選定，極其嚴格，申請者資格，須學業成績在八十五分以上，操行優良，而家庭狀况經調查後確屬淸寒者，始准參與老試。同時有一點要注意的，就是巳得他處助學金或獎學金的學生，不能再得叔蘋公獎學金。他們對於調查家庭狀况的工作，極爲認眞，冬日冒着風寒，夏天當着烈日，東西奔走，不辭辛苦，這種熱心社會事務的精神，眞足令人欽佩。

錄取的學生，由該獎學金資助學費，成績好的，並得資助書籍費，該獎學金對於學生課業，極爲注意，規定學生於每月十日以前，必須作學業狀况月報一次，報告他們的學習心得與生活狀况。這樣不但能督促學生努力求學，并且還可以知道學生的生活情形，至於學　成績特佳，總平均在九十五分以上的學生，尚有一學期膳宿費津貼的獎勵，由此可見叔蘋公獎學金對資助淸寒學生的熱心了。

除此之外，叔蘋公獎學金還　請高明醫生替學生檢查體格和免費治療，使學生的健康得以保障，以免一旦罹疾，忍受輾轉呻吟之苦。所以叔蘋公獎學金不但幫助學生解決升學的困雞，並且還盡量替學生解決生活上的痛苦，使他們能安心向學。

靑年學生是國家的柱石，培植靑年就是培養國家的精華，救濟淸寒學生升學雖然是艱苦的工作，可是它的意義是積極的，它對於社會的貢獻是偉大的，叔蘋公獎學金主辦人顧乾麟先生能以他全部的精神，寳貴的時間與金錢貢獻於救濟淸寒學生的偉大工作上，社會上實屬不可多見。希望社會上富有的人士也都能效法顧先生熱心服務的精神，多做一些有益社會的事業。我和顧公父子，素不相識，寫此文的目的，完全出於自動，只爲欽佩他的助學精神，現方經辦本報讀者助學金工作，更要惕勵奮勉忠誠服務。（酬助濟貧金）

How to Control Prices?

Yesterday, we discussed and offered suggestions regarding price control measures. Now, we will further discuss several key points that the authorities, business community, and the general public should pay attention to in managing and controlling prices, as follows:

First of all, we always believe that economic issues are part of political issues. If the political foundation is sound and the institutions are robust, any economic measures will naturally be easy to implement. Conversely, it will be difficult and ineffective, achieving half the result with twice the effort. Even with good laws and intentions, it is often difficult to achieve the desired results. In the past, the various controls implemented by the government authorities, initially legislated with good intentions, often resulted in abuses. Why? Because they suffered from an unsound political system. The general public often attributes the ineffectiveness of government actions to the unsoundness of institutions. However, why are the institutions unsound? It should still be acknowledged that it is due to a poor political foundation. Frankly speaking, in recent years, our people have only heard many beautiful slogans and touching banners. Many political problems have seen little reform. Currently, many undeniable morbid conditions are not only due to the unsoundness of institutions but also because the fundamental political issues are deeply ingrained and difficult to reverse with just a piece of paper or an order. Therefore, when considering the comprehensive control of prices in the future, we hope that everyone will work together to eliminate the root causes of past political problems. Otherwise, it will be a great obstacle to the future of control.

Therefore, we can point out that although controlling prices is about controlling goods, controlling goods must first start with controlling people, especially those who control people. If those who control people are well-controlled, then the general public can be expected to be well-controlled, and then goods can be expected to be well-controlled. When it comes to the issue of prices, we often denounce unscrupulous merchants who disrupt prices. In reality, so-called unscrupulous merchants are probably people with influence and connections in society. They have their ways. Therefore, unscrupulous merchants must be suppressed, and officials engaging in business must be strictly prohibited. The government already has a ban on civil servants engaging in commercial activities. This law must now be thoroughly implemented. The government should investigate which officials are concurrently serving as chairmen, directors, or managers of commercial banks or enterprises, or which merchants have become officials (especially in finance, economics, and control work). For these people with dual identities of officials and merchants, they should be ordered to resign from their official positions or business roles and specialize in one field. They should not be allowed to move freely and benefit from both sides. If the government can achieve this step and then provide strict training and assessment to personnel at all levels actually responsible for price control, the public's perception will be renewed, and significant results can be achieved in price control.

Secondly, regarding the organizations and personnel implementing price control, first, the implementing organizations must be unified. Because if the organizations are not unified, it will not only cause confusion for various industries and businesses but also lead to mutual constraints, evasions, and conflicts, which will inevitably hinder work efficiency. This must be carefully considered for comprehensive price management. Second, the leading authorities must adopt a supportive attitude towards industry, agriculture, and mining. A piece of paper or an order cannot solve the problem. If punishment is used lightly, it will make those engaged in production feel helpless. How can we expect an increase in production? Especially as mentioned in this newspaper a few days ago, China is a country dominated by small and medium-sized producers. The characteristics of small production are dispersion and weakness. The right remedy is to support their development and production, economically stimulate their interest in expanding production, and provide financial assistance for their capacity to expand production. This can solve the price problem from a positive perspective by increasing production. Third, those handling control work must act with conscience, be serious, and apply the rules without exception. A common problem in Chinese society is emphasizing relationships over law. Everyone takes pride in not being bound by laws and only expects others to abide by the law, especially powerful local officials and wealthy and unscrupulous gentry. They usually try their best to get by through flexibility and non-compliance. National laws often cannot be implemented effectively. These people are the culprits. In the future, we must pay close attention to eradicating this bad habit. Fourth, in terms of the implementing agencies, superiors should take more responsibility for their subordinates, providing guidance tirelessly, supervision as strict as possible, and reviews as frequent as possible. If the lower-level agencies implement policies improperly or cause abuses, the upper-level agencies should not only be held responsible for negligence but also bear joint responsibility.

Price control must be comprehensive and thorough. However, it cannot rely solely on laws and documents or the efforts of the authorities alone. It requires the united effort of the entire nation to be effective. Here, the first requirement is "openness." To the top and bottom, there must be thoroughness, no concealment, no cover-up, revealing the truth in everything, and announcing the real situation everywhere. By handling things openly, we can eliminate factors that allow for corruption through secrecy and gradually cultivate social disciplinary forces because all controls and punishments are made public to society, making everyone ashamed of non-compliance. Third, high-ranking government officials need to come forward and set an example of compliance with the law. The Chinese social tradition emphasizes relationships over principles, and the "inherent morality" of bending the law for personal connections really cannot exist today. It must start with high-ranking officials, emphasizing reason and abiding by the law. If there are exemplary cases of upholding justice even against family members in the government, there will be admirable cases of breaking away from personal relationships in society. Changing customs and practices and working together to overcome difficulties is an urgent task today, and it is not only relevant to the issue of prices.

My Suggestions for Winter Relief

— Rong Boyun

The time for winter relief is already pressing, so it is extremely urgent to organize a joint association of charitable organizations. To this end, I am calling on all sectors of society and proposing the following methods. I will briefly explain my humble opinion for my comrades to adopt.

(1) Benefits of Organizing a Joint Association of Winter Relief Charitable Organizations: The disadvantages of charitable organizations not being connected and cooperating are threefold: 1. Weak manpower results in a narrow scope of work and higher expenses; 2. Unsustainable organization makes it difficult to gain social trust and support from the majority; 3. The distribution of money and goods is concentrated in bustling areas, while the poor in remote areas are mostly left out, resulting in uneven distribution of hardship and happiness. After organizing the joint association, talents can be concentrated, fundraising can be divided and assigned, and relief distribution can be divided by region. The aforementioned three disadvantages can be avoided. To achieve the above-mentioned goals, we should immediately publish an announcement in the newspaper to convene all relevant winter relief charitable organizations and enthusiastic public welfare people from the industrial and commercial sectors to organize the establishment of a general office for the joint association. Each organization should elect enthusiastic people to preside over everything to promote progress and complete the relief mission.

(2) Methods of Fundraising: (A) If fundraising relies solely on automatic donations from all sectors of society, we can only awaken the wealthy to make everyone understand that contributing money to help the poor is a great and worthy act. The benefits will not only go to the poor, but the donor and their descendants will receive good rewards that are multiplied a hundred or a thousand times compared to the amount donated. This is true savings. Ordinary savings left to descendants are often not guaranteed. This kind of savings is one hundred and twenty percent reliable. (B) The relief association should submit a request to the authorities to approve the entrustment of all Baojia offices in the city to solicit relief donations from each household (one residence is counted as one household) on a voluntary basis. Each household should be required to donate 20 yuan from their New Year's food budget for winter relief expenses. The Baojia offices will collect and remit the funds to the relief association. Based on the total population of 4 million in Shanghai, with every 40 people forming one household, there would be 100,000 households, and the income should be 2 million yuan. The total donations from items A and B may reach 3 million yuan.

(3) Survey Steps for Relief Distribution: To make the survey procedures quick and universal, we should also entrust all Baojia offices in Shanghai to do it voluntarily. Provide emergency relief to the homeless and gradual relief to the hidden poor. The homeless originally have no household registration, so they should be given tickets stating "〇〇 Bao Homeless Person No. 〇〇 with name, gender and age", so that they can claim the relief based on the ticket, which is fair and universal. The survey should be conducted by all Baojia offices in Shanghai on the same day and at the same time. If the authorities can assist by cutting off all traffic in Shanghai for half an hour, it would be even better to prevent one person from registering in two places to obtain two homeless certificates.

(4) Headquarters for Collection and Custody of Property: It should be located at a suitable place to establish the General Office of the Shanghai Charitable Organization Joint Association. The responsibility for income and expenditure accounting and custody should be undertaken by voluntary personnel appointed by the Accountants Association.

If the above four items can be handled properly step by step, the poor and needy will benefit more than usual at present, and the Relief Association can still move further and move towards a more active path, engaging in reclamation and production and opening factories and other enterprises for thorough relief. This is the great fortune of the poor in Shanghai and also a major issue for improving society. Are these humble words feasible? I respectfully ask all benevolent leaders to make a public decision.
