鈴木首相素描

組閣的大命下降於鈴木貫太郎大將時，他正負着樞密院議長的重責。當時，就起來眞是無禮，竟有人發出「以七十九歲的老軀猶站在國民陣頭嗎」的不安之聲。除出道位至誠盡忠的人以外，無人能够擔當日本今日的局而。日本國民知道了鈴木大將是這樣一位人物時，纔放下了心。背負着日本時局的老宰相鈴木貫大郎大將，自從組閣以來，每天是怎樣奮鬥過來的呢？得到日木一億國民衷心的聲援而想引導此戰勝利的，是七十九歲的鈴木貫太郎大將。

首相的風格

不論誰會過鈴木貫太郎大將以後，都會感到他不是大眾的政治家。他旣不是所謂「大眾的」，也不是「政治家」，而是一位眞正的大將。今日就任首相以後，最能得人心的，也就是道一點。過去因爲沒有機會和民眾接觸，所以就任首相以後，如何觸及國民的心底，如何把握國民的呼聲，就成爲鈴木首相最深思苦慮的一點。這是接近鈴木氏的人說的。鈴木首相頗願坦懷聽取國民率直的意見和要求。自從組閣以來，鈴木首相正在不聲不饗中繼續邁進。

鈴木大將任首相以後，第一次出現於街頭是四月十五日的視察東京災區。當時，祗帶了一名秘書。四月十三日夜的空製，小石川的首相私邸附近，化爲一片火海。由於敬慕首相的人們以及警察官決死的消防活動，在廣大的火災區中，保全了相府及其鄰近區域，然而也曾一度極其危險。當時鈴木大將正在大塚某空地，混在一班首都市民中，互相策勵，一同避在防空壕裏。這時燭國燒夷彈紛落如雨，誰也不知道那個老人竟是總理大臣，直到一個國民學校的敎師喊道：「啊呀，那不是鈴木先生嗎！」大家方才認識。

那天晚上，鈴木首相當然整夜未睡，但在十四日上午四時半，他照常到官邸去辦公，而且比往日還要忙一點。那天晚上的廣播，也出於首相自已的意思。廣播以後，他覺得還不夠，因而又有第二天的巡視災區之舉。從官邸出發時，首相說：「眞想送一點什麼禮物給災民呢！」可是平素過着極儉樸的生活的首相家庭，一時實在找不出什麼適當的物品。「那末就把我這個點心送給他們，盡力去安慰一下災民吧？」

秘書官會談

足以表現首相希望實行儂貼民心的政治的，有一個會談。這就是每天下午九時起首相必和鈴木一。鈴木武。西村直己三秘書官會談。在鈴木大將任首相以前，只要沒有特別的事情，凡希望面會者，都可很快的得到會晤的機會；這是鈴木大將家的慣例。就任首相以後，鈴木大將曾對他的秘書們說道：

「我願會見任何人，聽無論那一個的話。」

不過，首相當然沒有接見每一個人的道理，因此有時是由秘書官代爲接見的。這三位秘書官，每夜將日裹所聞見的一切不分巨細的報告老宰相。自從鈴木內閣誕生以來，寄呈首相官邸的信札，條陳，逐日不下百通，首相自然也無這樣多的閒暇一一閱其內容，而由秘書官代拆轉告。每夜的會談，因此總在一小時以上。

必勝之決心

上午五時起床，下午十時就寢，這是鈴木大將的日課，但任首相以來，這個規律是打破了。就職時的第一聲：「願埋老軀於國民諸君的最前列！」這決不是一句形容詞。兩大三天泰然徹夜不睡的那種精神，似已趕走了七十九歲的高齡。側近的人雖勸他「勿過分辛勞」，首相卻仍舊精神嬰鑠，不以爲意。據其側近的一位說，那種勸吿是會玷汚首相決死的心理的

重慶六全大會評述

— 朱克家

重慶國民黨於（五月五日）起，召開第六屆全國代表大會，似乎以討論戰爭結束後的政治　領爲中心。就此間所可得見的消息觀察，其主要議題，將集中於　召集國民大會之具體問題，　五五憲章之最後決定問題，　修改國民黨政綱問題案等。從中國國民黨五十年來艱苦當鬥的歷史來看，除了民國十三年在孫中山先生親自領導下的一全大會以外，自以這次「六全大會」意義較爲車大，因爲預定討論的上述三項問題，對內對外各方面，都爲客觀上的重要關係。但是，這是照戯台上標貼的節目單所作的觀察，實際上戯還是戯，但看禍去多少次同樣的連台好戯，台上演唱得儘管精彩百出，台下監管彩聲雷動，結果對於國家民族的利益又有什麼成效可言，老百姓今天對於任何好戲已失去魅力，所期待的不是演唱的動聽，而是拯救國家命脈的實踐！

至於實踐問題，過去使國人失望的舊事不必車提了，就以今日重慶「六全大會」的主題來說：召集國民大會原是二十年來國民所共同盼望著的，但是所得到的只是聽聽黨員朗誦總理遺囑中的動人字句，和當局再三的失信。現在縱然決定舉行，但是國民如何推派眞正的代表？代表如何行使其職權而制定國家基本大法。重慶國民黨政機顯然沒有令人滿意的答覆。誠如重慶的中國民主政團同盟宣言（本年一月三十日）所說：「如僅僅將二十五·六年所選出之一部份代表，再就無法選舉之若干省份指派若干代表，符合一千四百四十名額，更益以數百名國民黨中委及候補中委爲當然委員，以此而欲制定一部全國共遵之憲法，以此而居然言還致於民，並欲以此而成就全國的團結統一，吾人認爲其結果必將廻得其反」。其實，民主政團同盟中人，難道還不知重慶當局的用心所在？實際上，中國人民莫不渴望早日召開眞正的全體的國民代表大會，商決國是，實行憲政，但是必待全國和平統一之後，才有實現可能，否則，就是實現了，也只是全國不足三分之一地域一部份人的意見，將來決不能解決整個的國是，徒增未來的紛歧而已。

持平而論，重慶召集的「六全大會」，實際上未能消除個人及黨派的私心，因此未能獲得國民之擁護與信任，是意想中事。我們必須鄭重指出：「大道之行，天下爲公」，現在重慶國民黨的六全大會，只知巧立名目，實岡保持及擴展個人與私黨的權利，常然不會有何成效的前途。然而縱觀大局，嚴果已極，國人若不迅圖全國和平統一，則內戰外患，兵連禍結，巾華民族，沉淪堪膚。深願全國上下，其速猛省！脚踏實地，統一團結，爲中國爲東亞爭取生存。

Ministry of Finance Announces: Gold Certificate Issuance Outline

According to reliable sources from the Ministry of Finance, regarding the Central Reserve Bank's issuance of gold certificates, the Ministry of Finance has drafted the "Gold Certificate Issuance Outline" and the "Organizational Regulations of the Gold Certificate Issuance Reserve Management Committee." These have been submitted to the Executive Yuan, which in turn submitted them to the National Government and the Central Political Committee for approval and record. They were officially promulgated and implemented by the Ministry of Finance on May 5th. The aforementioned "Gold Certificate Issuance Outline" and "Organizational Regulations of the Gold Certificate Reserve Management Committee" are excerpted below:

Gold Certificate Issuance Outline

(Article 1) In order to maintain the value of currency, the Ministry of Finance specially permits the Central Reserve Bank to issue gold certificates.

(Article 2) The face value of the gold certificates will be expressed in the weight of gold, with the unit being "tael," and divided into three types: one tael, five taels, and ten taels.

(Article 3) Gold certificates are payable to the bearer upon presentation. They are not registered, and the following items should be clearly stated on the certificate: (1) Issuing institution and issuance number. (2) Weight and fineness. (3) Sale date and redemption date. (4) Redemption location.

(Article 4) Gold certificates may be sold in the market and may be entrusted to reliable financial institutions as agents for sale.

(Article 5) Regarding the issuance of gold certificates, the sales period, sale price, redemption date, and other items shall be decided and announced by the Central Reserve Bank at any time with reference to market conditions.

(Article 6) Regarding the gold required for the redemption of gold certificates, a Gold Certificate Issuance Reserve Management Committee will be established to handle its safekeeping. The organizational regulations will be formulated separately.

(Article 7) A special vault will be established to store the reserve for gold certificates.

(Article 8) Those who counterfeit or sell gold certificates will be punished according to the "Provisional Regulations for Punishing Wartime Counterfeiting of Legal Tender."

(Article 9) These measures will be implemented from the date of promulgation.

Organizational Regulations of the Gold Certificate Issuance Reserve Management Committee

(Article 1) The Gold Certificate Issuance Reserve Management Committee is organized in accordance with the provisions of Article 6 of the Gold Certificate Issuance Outline.

(Article 2) The committee will be located at the Shanghai branch of the Central Reserve Bank.

(Article 3) The committee will consist of seven to ten members. The president of the Central Reserve Bank will be the chairman of the committee, the vice president will be the vice-chairman, and other members will be appointed or hired by the Minister of Finance from the following personnel: "Leaders of commercial organizations," "Individuals of established reputation."

(Article 4) The committee will have a number of staff and clerks.

(Article 5) The committee will meet once a month, and at the end of each month, it shall submit the following items to the Ministry of Finance and announce them publicly: (1) The number of gold certificates issued. (2) The situation of gold certificate sales. (3) The quantity of gold redeemed. (4) The quantity of issuance reserve. (5) Other necessary matters.

(Article 6) These regulations will be implemented from the date of promulgation.

Introducing the Nanshi People's Hospital

— Xi

The "Nanshi People's Hospital," as the name suggests, reveals its location and characteristics. In fact, this hospital is located close to the city center at 775 Minquan Road, near Zhengjia Wood Bridge. The property of the hospital was formerly the Tongyi Benevolent Association Building, which was once used as the "Green Treasure Gambling Hall." Transportation is very convenient. It is not only the largest and most well-equipped hospital in Nanshi at present, but also the only large hospital in the southeast corner of Huangpu District. It is a pity that it is not well-known.

Establishment Process

The hospital was founded in the late spring of the thirty-second year of the Republic [1943]. At that time, public-spirited individuals recognized that Nanshi had not yet recovered from the devastation of the war, and all facilities were mostly rudimentary. The residents of the area were mostly people with weak financial resources, and with the rising prices, ordinary people found it increasingly difficult to bear the burden of medical expenses. Therefore, every summer, epidemics bred, and they were helpless. Thus, the establishment of the Nanshi Epidemic Hospital was proposed to supplement the shortage of hospitals in the area. The hospital was initially set up at Sanle Ying, Yifeng Lane, Zhaojia Road. The layout was roughly arranged, and diagnosis and treatment were immediately provided. From summer to autumn and winter, it saved the lives of five to six thousand civilians. Later, as more people became aware of it and the number of patients increased, the hospital site was insufficient. Furthermore, due to the lack of a fully equipped hospital in the area, the hospital's board of directors resolved to expand it into a people's hospital.

After moving to the new site in January of the thirty-fourth year [1945], the buildings were renovated. The entire hospital has more than eighty rooms, with good air circulation and ample light, which is very suitable for medical use. The chairman of the board is Wang Yongkang, and the president of the hospital is Jin Xuegao. There are ten doctors, sixteen nurses, six interns, and twelve hospital staff. The director of the medical department is Zheng Weiru, under whom are divided: Internal Medicine, Pediatrics, Surgery, Tuberculosis, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Dermatology, Ophthalmology, Addiction Treatment, Otolaryngology, Physical Therapy, and Dispensing (double prescription) departments. The director of the general affairs department is Xu Weiyu, under whom are divided: Document Department, General Affairs Section, and Accounting Section.

Complete Equipment

The expanded hospital has more than forty wards, with a total of more than one hundred and twenty beds. Specialized doctors in various departments were also newly hired, with experts in charge. Nurses were added, X-ray machines were purchased, and a surgery room was newly opened, equipped with the latest German-made operating table, electric hemostatic device, etc. The laboratory is also equipped with extremely precise instruments. The important equipment of the hospital is almost all available, which is much more complete than the small hospitals in the city.

Free Diagnosis and Treatment

The hospital provides completely free medical treatment for civilians from 9:00 AM to 11:30 AM. From 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM, a small consultation fee is charged: 100 yuan for the first visit, 50 yuan for a follow-up visit, 200 yuan for an emergency visit, and 400 yuan for a house call. For ward meals, first-class is 2,400 yuan per day with two meals and one porridge, second-class is 2,200 yuan per day, second-class is 1,800 yuan per day with two meals and one porridge, and third-class is 1,000 yuan per day with two porridge and one meal. Delivery hospitalization for one week, including medical expenses, surgery fees, and room and board, costs 10,000 yuan. Those with difficult financial situations can request free medical treatment. Therefore, monthly expenses are about 4 million yuan, and the income is far from sufficient. We hope that people from all walks of life will actively donate funds to expand the people's medical fund.
