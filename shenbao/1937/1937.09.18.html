淸算六年來的血賬

— 賓

“九一八”的六周年來臨了。

“九一八。”這慘痛的一九一八。”這恥辱的“九一八。”在沉默的苦悶中悄悄地過去了六年。在以往的六年中每年到了這一天。我們甚至不能公開痛快的來紀念它。我們熾盛的怒火只能在內心裏燒。我們悲憤的血淚只能在暗地裏流。

然而在今天。情形却完全不同了。我們四萬五千萬的偉大民族已經怒吼起來。我們神聖的全民抗戰已經轟轟烈烈地展開了。在華北。在華中。我們英勇的戰士前仆後繼的正在向敵人厮殺。我全國同胞誰都下了抗戰到底的决心。六年以来。我們東北以及冀察一部五千萬的同胞受够了敵人的凌辱。我們的主權和領士受够了敵人的蹂躏。我們的文化受够了敵人的摧殘。到今天。我們要跟敵人來淸算這六年來的血賬了。

日本帝國主義一贯的侵略行為。六年以來。沒有一天停止過。從遼寧吉林黑龍江的被佔領。熱河的淪陷。冀東二十二縣偽政府的成立。華北的特殊化。豐台的被佔以至盧溝橋事件的爆發。都是日帝國主義侵略的成果。日帝國主義侵略我國的領土是决沒有止境的。

而且。伴着軍事侵略的進展。日帝國主義還用了種種毒辣的方法來打擊我們。它嗾使日韓浪人進行大規模的走私。以破壤我們經濟的命脈。它利用失意政客和軍閥。以分化我們的中央政府和地方當局。它甚至在華北各地遍設吸毒機關毒化中國。為的是要消滅我們中華民族的人種。其他如奴化教育的實施。“特務機關”的遍設無一不是企圖滅亡中國的狠毒的陰謀。

這一大篇的血賬。現在是到了總淸算的時候了。中華四萬五千萬的人民。不論階級。不論黨派。已在“抗敵”的目標下像鐵一樣地團結起來。我們要用血來洗雪我們的恥辱。來拯救我們被奴役的五千萬同胞。來收回我們的失地。來爭取我們整個民族的生存。

“九一八”六周年了。我國的歷史開始寫到最光榮最燦爛的一頁。兩個月來抗戰的結束證明了我們的戰鬥力量並不弱。證明了我們士氣的旺盛遠勝於敵人。證明了國際的同情都是屬於我們這方面。我們已有了更大的自信。我們將更堅定更持久的抗戰下去。非至奪回我們全部的失地。洗雪我們的恥辱。誓不罷休。今年的“九一八”還不過是我們神聖抗敵的起點。明年今天。我門相信總可以到瀋陽去跟東北的同胞“額手相慶”了。

— 本報特派員叔棣

㊅卑鄙戰略

敵人方面的習慣，一碰到記述他們自己的所謂“皇軍”時，往往就加上“堂堂”這兩個字樣。自從敵人在華北的駐屯軍，大量增加以後，這所謂“皇軍”，給予人們的印𧰼，也就一天一天地，加多了起來，深刻了起來，然而，每一個住在平津的人，祇要他回憶一下對於敵軍的印𧰼，那首先想起來的，大概是這幾項：星期日的時候，全付武裝，成大隊地，開到公共塲所去逛；在街上常常為一大枚兩大枚銅子的爭執，怒吼着，向洋車夫拔出刺刀；一碰到演習後，駕着戰車飛馳過市的時候，那端着槍，怒目而視的姿態，幾乎隨時隨地，要把子彈打進人的胸膛；……等等。然而，在這些印𧰼裏，却絲毫也發現不出近似他們自己所謂的“堂堂”。

敵軍這回在華北和我們二十九軍作戰，一般人都知道，他們武器的精銳，是許多倍於二十九軍的。然而，卽在戰略上，他們也有足述的處所。比如，戰鬥裏，他們的人數，往往超過或至少等於我們方面的部隊，才敢作戰。二十九軍號稱十多萬，然而，總是枝枝節節的應戰；敵人宣稱進攻三十七師了，於是三十八師不動；敵人宣稱進攻的是蘆溝橋，而近在咫尺的北平，還在希圖茍安。說起來，二十九軍人數遠過敵軍，而實際，在每次戰鬥裏，却不見得就比他們多。不用說，這是中了敵人“各個擊破”的戰略。此外，敵人又利用中日關係的畸形，而到處應用着“半眞半假”的卑鄙戰略；明明是通知我方，說是“通過”或“換防”的，然而，看到在某處顯足以擊敗我軍時，那就會立刻停下來襲擊；明明是取着進攻姿態的，然而，一等到近處，看見我軍勢不可侮時，也會立刻換了態度，說是根據條約，開赴某處“換防”；明明先是說“換防”後來變成攻擊，然而，戰到中間，看到形勢不利了，也會忽然老着面皮，說是“雙方衝突，由於誤會”，要求停戰，讓他們通過了。比如彰儀門戰爭那一次，先是日軍要求開門，讓他們進城，開入兵營；後來隊伍進了一半時，忽然對守軍加以襲擊；再後來，經我軍包圍後，又由身任冀察顧問的日人櫻井等，出面制止我軍射擊，而讓他們開進東交民巷的兵營。結果，櫻井是被我軍憤怒地由城墻上摜到街上摔死了；然而，那些被包圍的殘敵，却終於逃脫殲滅，安然放出回營。……總而言之，為了達到戰勝目的，一切卑劣的手段，總不惜採取的。這就是日軍這回在華北作戰的戰略。

其次，敵軍戰鬥情緒的低落，是可驚的。肉搏戰是他們的制命傷，我們軍隊祇要能衝到他們的面前，其意義，就等於他們的潰敗。他們時時刻刻記在心裏的，唯一的秘訣，就是：“遠離華軍，遠離華軍。”在大砲·機槍的射程之內，他們儘可能地遠離着華軍，於是，身子伏在戰壕裏，大砲·機槍·飛機·炸彈，這些，有時超過必要地亂用着，簡直成了浪費。設若，有一連敵軍被圍着，已經消滅了一半，那末，祇要圍他們的中國軍，是超過他們一倍的；這時，這殘餘的敵人，一準會自動繳械，願為俘虜，茍全性命。從前聽說，日本武士道，是每個人愛惜他的武器，超過生命，至死不肯放鬆的；但這回在華北的表現，却是，一到危急，就拿武器去換取生命。同時，因戰鬥情緒低落而起的神經過敏，也發揮到了極端。比如，二十九軍自北平退出後，北平四郊，有少數的冀北保安隊還沒有完全退走，時不時，零零星星地，給敵軍以打擊。這，使敵人麻煩了許多天。一碰到老百姓，就逼着他招出保安隊藏在什麼地方；說“沒有”麼，那末，這又令敵人懷疑到，這個人本身，也許就是保安隊。凡是年輕力壯的，剃着光頭的，穿着汗背心的，穿黃褲子的，………等等，在敵人眼裏，總有很大的保安隊嫌疑。遇到時，略有言語不淸，立刻就加以屠殺，甚至爲萬全起見，連問也不敢問，遠遠地就給以一槍。又比如，八月八號進城以後，住的地方，一共有三處：鐵獅子胡同的綏靖公署舊址，旃檀寺，天壇。這三處，原都是住軍的兵營，構築非常穩固。而况，這時的北平城裏，除敵軍外，誰也沒槍了。然而，敵軍還是不放心的，每到夜晚，步哨要放到二三里路以外的大街，還時時開着機關槍，放着步槍，“像煞有介事”地防着夜襲。諸如此類神經過敏的處所，是非常之多的。而藏在一切底裏的，還祇是“懦怯”。

八號進北平城那天，據所謂“大日本軍北平入城司令”的佈告，入城目的，是“端在襄助維持治安。”然而進城的第一著，就是收繳督察保安隊的槍械，第二著，就是運走二十九軍在平時所存的軍糧，為了提防路上搶刦，還一面運，一面在糧軍後面放槍，第三著，就是把所有重要地方的門口，都換上日軍的崗位，大概到必要時，就把存物運走。故宮是不能搬走的，但那些騰下的古物，恐怕要東渡了。在城外鄉下的時候，每到一處就逼老百姓代找年輕女子，送到軍營裏去，這舉動，在城裏還沒有過，大概因為各國觀瞻所繫的緣故吧，滿載着兵士的小型摩托車，大街上到處皆是。這些車子，速率開得很慢，遇到吃食担子，或小飯舖子之類，往往就停下來，吃點東西再走。吃的時候，先逼着買食品的人，當面吃給他們看，然後，才敢放心地吃下肚去。未吃之前，先問問價錢，一毛不拔而白吃的事，我沒有目覩過；我常常目見到的，却是：一個日兵問價之後，不管三七二十一，就連連搖頭道：“大不便宜！大不便宜！”另一個他的同夥，就硬作主張地豎起指頭，給一個價。自然賣也要賣；不賣也要賣。因為，他們一邊說，一面在摸着槍的。吃後所付的，却是日本票子。

有些人，正在街上走着的時候，忽然有輛日軍的汽車開到面前了。車子裏一聲斷喝，於是，這個人就被強迫着去洗刷汽車。

日人自己所謂“堂堂皇軍”的，其在華北的表現，就是這一類。

Yesterday's Heavy Rain Made Combat Difficult, Only Minor Engagements on All Fronts

Enemy's Heavy Armaments Ineffective; Fierce Encounter in Our Counterattack on Yangxing; Enemy Attempted Another Attack on Pudong, Repelled by Our Forces

After a night and day of bloody fighting in villages near Luodian, the stubborn enemy was repelled by our forces. From yesterday afternoon until this morning, there were no major battles due to the rain. Our front line remained stable. In the Liuhang area, the enemy concentrated artillery fire, but our positions suffered no damage. In the Jiangwan-Zhabei area, there were minor skirmishes last night, with clear machine gun and rifle fire, but the enemy was unsuccessful. The enemy's plan to break out of Luodian and attack our Liuhang failed. Currently, enemy ships are concentrating, seemingly reconnoitering Liuhang, Yanglinkou, and Qiyakou. According to confirmed reports, the enemy ships originally anchored in the Songkou area have dispersed and anchored in the river areas of Shidong, Liuhang, Yanglinkou, and Qiyakou, with the largest concentration in Liuhang. The total number of enemy ships anchored in these areas has reached approximately fifty. Enemy ships in Yanglinkou and Qiyakou shelled our riverside positions intermittently throughout yesterday, seemingly as a probe. Our forces were well-prepared and resolved to give the enemy a head-on blow.

Yesterday was a day of torrential rain. Although there were continuous local engagements along the Luodian, Yangxing, and Jiangwan lines, the rain made military operations difficult for both sides, and the scope could not be expanded. The enemy's heavy armaments could not exert their power. The fields were muddy and slippery, rendering the enemy's tanks completely ineffective. Due to the rain, enemy aircraft activity on the front lines was greatly reduced. Yesterday, the enemy could only bombard our positions aimlessly with artillery from the rear, with no other possible activity. All shells landed in the mud and lost their explosive force. Our forces' counterattacks on all lines were temporarily suspended, and there were no changes in the positions.

Our Counterattack on Yangxing Succeeds, Recapturing Important Villages

Fierce Artillery Battles Erupted Between Both Sides Yesterday

Our counterattack on Yangxing was quite successful, and we have completely recaptured the important villages on both sides. The vanguard has advanced to the outskirts of Yangxing. Yesterday, the advance was slightly delayed due to the rain. The enemy artillery tried to stop our advance, bombarding our rear positions in Liuhang and Yong'anqiao indiscriminately throughout the day with incessant artillery fire. The artillery fire became even denser in the afternoon, roaring endlessly, and shaking the surrounding areas. Our artillery also returned fire, and the artillery battle subsided slightly towards evening. Last night, a fierce artillery battle erupted again.

After occupying Yangxing, the enemy continued to advance along the highway, attacking Yong'anqiao in an attempt to assault our Liuhang positions. The day before yesterday afternoon, under the cover of our heavy artillery in the rear, we launched a full-scale counterattack against the enemy, advancing simultaneously on both the left and right flanks. The mud and mire after the rain rendered the enemy's tanks useless. Our forces seized the opportunity to advance rapidly eastward, causing heavy casualties to the enemy, who retreated in disarray. Our forces then recaptured the important villages nearby, such as Fengjiazhai, Shenjiazhai, and Xishenjiazhai, one after another, and continued to press towards Yangxing along the highway. By evening, we had advanced to the vicinity of Yangxing. That night, the enemy launched a fierce counterattack against our forces, attempting to recapture the positions. Our forces fought bravely, relying on the strong defenses, and repelled the enemy's repeated assaults. Fierce fighting continued yesterday morning. By noon, the counterattacking enemy began to retreat.

Enemy's Plan to Attack Liuhang Fails

Due to the setbacks on the Luodian front and the pressure of our vehicles advancing on Yangxing, the enemy had no choice but to change their strategy, advancing from the central Jiangwan route in an attempt to break through our southeastern positions in that area. The night before last, they concentrated artillery fire, bombarding our Jiangwan positions incessantly throughout the day, attempting to cover their troops' advance. Our forces were well-prepared and heavily defended. Their artillery fire was completely ineffective due to the rain. Our defending troops calmly fought back, and when the enemy approached our positions, machine gunners and grenade launchers suddenly opened fire simultaneously. The enemy was caught off guard, and those who could not escape were sacrificed under the machine guns. Our forces jumped up and swept forward, and the enemy collapsed. Yesterday's heavy rain silenced the fighting along that line.

The Central News Agency reported that the enemy's operational plan for the past two days seemed to be an attempt to break through our southeastern lines in the Jiangwan area, attack Liuhang, and encircle the right flank of our forces in the Luodian area. However, the results were all valiantly repelled by our forces, and the enemy was unsuccessful.

Enemy's Attack on the Patriotic Women's School Repelled

The enemy's naval landing force from the Hongkou area, aided by regular army troops, again advanced along Jiangwan Road towards the Patriotic Women's School the night before last under the cover of artillery fire from the enemy naval headquarters and Hongkou Park. This was discovered by our outpost soldiers, who lay in ambush behind defensive structures and waited for them to approach before unleashing a dense barrage of machine gun fire, killing many enemy soldiers. The enemy scattered and lay on the ground, engaging us in a stalemate that lasted all night, but they were ultimately unsuccessful.

Our Forces in Zhabei Launch a Fierce Assault on the Stubborn Enemy

The night before last, our garrison in Zhabei, taking advantage of the clear skies after the fresh rain and the shining moon, launched a sudden and fierce attack with fresh troops along the line of enemy forces in Hongkou and Zhabei, preemptively. The enemy hid behind defensive structures,不敢出而抵抗, only firing artillery at our North Railway Station and the Zhabei area for a long time. By daybreak yesterday morning, the sky was lightly raining again, and our forces retreated to their original defenses, and the artillery fire also subsided.
