蘇聯能不能帮助我們？

— 胡愈之

蘇聯能不能幫助我們？要問答這一個問題，我們先得問一下自己！我們要不要蘇聯的帮助。

從主觀上說，我們爲求自力更生而抗戰到底，我們相信可以不倚賴一切外力，而得到最後的勝利。　所以我們不需要任何他國的帮助，所以　也不需要蘇聯的帮助。

但是從客觀上說，我們不懂是爲了我們自身的獨立自由而戰，也是爲了受日本侵略威脅的遠東各國的共同利益而戰，爲了全世界的和平興正義而戰。所以，一切友邦，不問是爲了他們自身的利益，或者爲了和平興正義，願意幫助我們，反對日本侵略戰爭，不懂是我們所不能拒絕的，而且也是我們所十分需要的。　假如友邦的援助，能夠使中國抗戰早一天得到勝利，能夠使日本侵略者早一天敗減，我們爲什麼竟不要友邦的帮助呢？

不過我們不能希望一切的友邦，都給我們有効的援助。有的國家，和我們的敵人，有了同盟關係，我們就不能希望得到眞正的帮助。有的國家，雖然願意帮助我們，可是或者因爲地理位置隔離的太遠，痛癢不相關，或者因爲把自身利益看得太重，不願意輕易冒險，或者因爲實力太不充足，我們也不能希望從這些國家，得到眞正有効的帮助。自然，各國人民對中國民族抗戰的同情，是完全一致的。可是單靠這些道義上的同情，斷不會使我們滿足。

因此我們可以希望得到的帮助，主要地自然是蘇聯。誰都知道，日本是蘇聯的共同敵人。爲了蘇聯本身所受的威脅，爲了世界和平興正義，蘇聯必須帮助我們。而且就地理位置，軍略形勢，軍備實力來說，除了英美法之外，也只有蘇聯，才能給予我們眞正有効的帮助。

所以在客觀上我們需耍蘇聯的帮助，是不成問題的，問題都在於蘇聯能不能帮助我們，什麼時候才能帮助我們。我相信現在有許多人都爲了這個問題煩悶着。因爲雖然中蘇兩國已經訂結了一個不侵犯條約，雖然李維諾夫在日內瓦發表了痛詆侵略戰爭的演說，雖然莫斯科已經爲了日本飛機轟炸中國城市提出了抗議，但是除此以外，蘇聯帮助中國抗戰的事實，還沒有具體表現；而莫斯科對中日問題的表面外交行動，也沒有超過倫敦和華盛頓的外交姿勢。（今天報載西伯利亞紅軍移動的消息，是例外）這些事實，卻不免使許多人懷疑苦悶起來。大家都問着；蘇聯到底能不能帮助我們呢？

許多人以爲蘇聯不能帮助我們的原因，第一由於蘇聯國內淸黨肅軍，使蘇聯實力削弱；第二由於蘇聯顧忌日本；第三由於蘇聯顧忌德國；第四由於蘇聯顧忌英國。提出第一種原因的，可以說完全不瞭解蘇聯國情。蘇聯淸黨肅軍的結果，是使紅軍組織更加堅强，對外實力更見增加。第二第三種原因，也不能成立。不錯，蘇聯在東西兩方面，同樣受到侵略的威脅。可是自從二次五年計畫成功之後，西伯利亞與白俄羅斯，鳥克蘭，防禦工臣，已堅固得和鈾牆鐵壁一樣，紅軍紀律和設備，也遠在一般想像之上。蘇聯國防是以東西邊境獨立配置的。換句話說，西境駐軍配備以德國波蘭爲假想敵，西伯利亞駐軍配備，以日本爲假想敵，足以分頭應付而有餘。所以蘇聯不但不畏懼對日作戰，卽使在東西兩方邊境，同時對德日兩國作戰，也有絕對的勝利把握。

至於說到蘇聯因顧忌英國，所以不能帮助我們，這也許近於事實。因爲英國對於參加歐洲法蘇陣綫和德意陳綫始終舉棋不定。假如英國不能提出保障，在德日進攻蘇聯時，决不帮助侵略國，那麼蘇聯能否冒險帮助中國，因而引起德意英日的聯合進攻，這誠然是值得考慮的。

直到目前爲止，英國保守黨內閣對於西歐和遠東和平陣綫和侵略陣綫的對立，依然沒有採取確定的態度，這可以說是使蘇聯對於遠東事變暫時緘默的唯一理由。但是這不過就目前的形勢來說。假如中國外交能採取更堅决的步驟，一面與日本絕交，一面拉攏蘇聯和美國，在那時，英國就不能再行模稜兩可，牠耍更堅决地來反對日本侵略中國。因爲英國是斷不能因爲討好日本而和中蘇美三國爲敵的。現在有好多人對於美國，抱悲觀態度。我却相信美國地位，和蘇聯差不多。美國所顧慮的也是英國。只要我們能夠在外交上表示堅决的反日立塲，美蘇兩國的態度，都會變更，共同壓迫英國，反對日本，而目前外交的僵局，也就打破了。

我們不必隱瞞，我們的抗戰决心，在外交行動上，似乎表現的太不夠。在軍事上我們已經有焦土抗戰的事實，在外交上我們却還沒有遮斷與日本妥洽的道路·我們至今並沒有和敵國斷絕邦交，而且在日內瓦也沒有堅决要求對日實施軍事經濟制裁·這顯然由於英國紳士外交的牽制，使抗日外交不能貫澈到底，在事實上雖然可以原諒。但是中蘇兩國關係，不能迅速進展，主要的原因，也就在這裏。

所以說到最後，蘇聯能不能帮助我們，這問題的解答，還耍靠我們自己。只耍我們自己能夠削減一切茍安的心理，無聊的幻想，向全世界堂堂正正表示抗日到底的决心，不但蘇聯能夠帮助我們，全世界愛和平正義的人士，也全耍站在我們這一邊。

因為要甄別各童子軍的能力和所受童子軍訓練的學識，當每一個童子軍來交登記表的時候，必須加以試騐，藉此知道他的個性能力學識禮節及其他，自二十五日起開始。

腥風

— □狷

大華烈士簡又文先生，每作隨筆雜感之類，往往因地域的區別，而以“東南風”“西北風”等為其標題，信手拈來，不失風人之旨。聞自我國全面抗戰發動以來，無論東風·西風·南風·北風中，全都充滿着血腥氣；因名我篇曰“腥風”。

我軍的淞滬新陣綫，雖天天遭受敵軍最猛烈的砲火炸彈不斷地轟擊，步兵騎兵和坦克車不斷地進攻；卻好似銅牆鐵壁一般，還是屹然未動，比了我們舊的萬里長城，堅固百倍，這眞如義勇軍進行曲中所謂把我們的血肉，築成我們新的長城了。

處治那萬惡的漢奸，最好是沿用舊律滿門抄斬；那麼家人骨肉之間，為了怕被株連起見，平日就得互相監視，大家不敢再做漢奸。要是就罪不及孥，滿門抄斬辦不到，我以為對於漢奸本身，也非斬首不可。不見德國處治賣國賊，近年來都用斬首之刑，雖婦女不能例外。所以呌窮兇極惡的漢奸身首異處。不能說是殘酷。槍斃，不但太便宜了漢奸，實在也太不經濟；省下每一顆子彈來，至少可以打死一個侵略我們的敵人。

上海日暉港遭敵機轟炸時，又炸死了我們許多無辜的平民。內中有一個孕婦，給彈片洞穿了肚子，那胎兒的小手，竟伸出肚外，一會兒自行產出，可是那小生命也已不保了。大家試閉上眼睛想一想，這是何等慘痛的情景！唉！可惜這孩子已死了；他要是活着的話，我願意牽着他那雙小手，踏進這血腥氣的世界，放在後方安全地帶，好好地撫育他起來，自幼兒就給他受那空軍的敎育，并時時使他看着他母親的遺影，朝夕加以激勵，把他造就一個勇猛無比的空軍戰士。到得一朝學成之後，就由他帶領着大隊空軍，渡海而東，投下千百個大炸彈去，把那扶桑二島，轟成一片白地。呵呵！這是多麼痛快的報復！

Heavy Fighting Erupts at Shenjiawan; Enemy Forces in Downtown Area Transferred to Liuhang

Our Frontline Troops Replenished and Well-Prepared for a Decisive Blow. Enemy Forces Attacked Zhabei Yesterday Morning but Were Repelled. Dachang, Pudong, and the South City Were Bombed by Enemy Aircraft Yesterday.

Can the Soviet Union Help Us?

— Hu Yuzhi

Can the Soviet Union help us? To answer this question, we must first ask ourselves: Do we need the Soviet Union's help?

Subjectively speaking, we are striving for self-reliance and fighting to the end, and we believe that we can achieve final victory without relying on any external forces. Therefore, we do not need any other country's help, and therefore, we do not need the Soviet Union's help either.

However, objectively speaking, we understand that we are not only fighting for our own independence and freedom but also for the common interests of the Far Eastern countries threatened by Japanese aggression, and for world peace and justice. Therefore, we cannot refuse the help of any friendly country, whether for their own interests or for peace and justice, who is willing to help us and oppose the Japanese war of aggression, and we also desperately need it. If the assistance of friendly countries can enable China's war of resistance to achieve victory one day earlier, and can enable the Japanese aggressors to be defeated one day earlier, why would we not want the help of friendly countries?

However, we cannot expect all friendly countries to give us effective assistance. Some countries have allied relations with our enemies, so we cannot hope to get real help. Some countries, although willing to help us, are either too far away geographically, so that they do not feel our pain, or they value their own interests too much and are unwilling to take risks lightly, or their strength is not sufficient. We cannot hope to get really effective help from these countries. Naturally, the sympathy of the people of all countries for the Chinese nation's war of resistance is completely unanimous. However, relying solely on these moral sympathies will never satisfy us.

Therefore, the help we can hope to receive is mainly from the Soviet Union. Everyone knows that Japan is the common enemy of the Soviet Union. For the sake of the threat to the Soviet Union itself, and for world peace and justice, the Soviet Union must help us. Moreover, in terms of geographical location, military strategy, and military strength, only the Soviet Union, in addition to Britain, the United States, and France, can give us truly effective help.

Therefore, objectively speaking, our need for the Soviet Union's help is not a problem. The problem lies in whether the Soviet Union can help us and when it can help us. I believe that many people are troubled by this question now. Because although China and the Soviet Union have already signed a non-aggression treaty, although Litvinov delivered a speech in Geneva denouncing wars of aggression, and although Moscow has protested against the bombing of Chinese cities by Japanese aircraft, apart from this, the facts of Soviet aid to China's war of resistance have not yet been concretely demonstrated; and Moscow's superficial diplomatic actions on the Sino-Japanese issue have not exceeded the diplomatic gestures of London and Washington. (Today's news about the movement of the Siberian Red Army is an exception.) These facts cannot help but make many people doubt and feel distressed. Everyone is asking: Can the Soviet Union really help us?

Many people believe that the reasons why the Soviet Union cannot help us are: first, the purges and military reorganizations in the Soviet Union have weakened the Soviet Union's strength; second, the Soviet Union is concerned about Japan; third, the Soviet Union is concerned about Germany; and fourth, the Soviet Union is concerned about Britain. Proposing the first reason shows a complete lack of understanding of the Soviet Union's national conditions. The result of the Soviet Union's purges and military reorganizations is that the Red Army organization has become stronger and its external strength has increased. The second and third reasons are also untenable. It is true that the Soviet Union is equally threatened by aggression from both the east and west. However, since the success of the Second Five-Year Plan, the defense works in Siberia, Belarus, Ukraine, have become as solid as copper walls and iron walls, and the discipline and equipment of the Red Army are also far beyond general imagination. Soviet national defense is configured independently on the eastern and western borders. In other words, the Western border garrison is equipped with Germany and Poland as the hypothetical enemies, and the Siberian garrison is equipped with Japan as the hypothetical enemy, which is sufficient to deal with them separately and have surplus capacity. Therefore, the Soviet Union is not only not afraid of fighting Japan, but even if it fights Germany and Japan simultaneously on both the eastern and western borders, it has absolute confidence in victory.

As for saying that the Soviet Union cannot help us because it is concerned about Britain, this may be close to the truth. Because Britain has always hesitated to participate in the European Franco-Soviet front and the German-Italian front. If Britain cannot provide guarantees that it will never help the aggressor countries if Germany and Japan attack the Soviet Union, then whether the Soviet Union can risk helping China, thereby triggering a joint attack by Germany, Italy, Britain, and Japan, is indeed worth considering.

Until now, the British Conservative cabinet has still not adopted a definite attitude towards the opposition between the Western European and Far Eastern peace fronts and the aggression fronts. This can be said to be the only reason for the Soviet Union's temporary silence on the Far Eastern incident. However, this is only in terms of the current situation. If Chinese diplomacy can take more resolute steps, on the one hand, severing relations with Japan, and on the other hand, wooing the Soviet Union and the United States, then Britain can no longer be ambiguous, and it will more resolutely oppose Japan's aggression against China. Because Britain can never make enemies of China, the Soviet Union, and the United States in order to please Japan. Now many people have a pessimistic attitude towards the United States. However, I believe that the United States' position is similar to that of the Soviet Union. What the United States is also worried about is Britain. As long as we can express a resolute anti-Japanese position in diplomacy, the attitudes of the United States and the Soviet Union will change, and they will jointly put pressure on Britain to oppose Japan, and the current diplomatic deadlock will be broken.

We do not have to hide that our determination to resist Japan seems to be insufficiently expressed in diplomatic actions. In military terms, we already have the fact of scorched-earth resistance, but in diplomacy, we have not yet blocked the road to reconciliation with Japan. We have not yet severed diplomatic relations with the enemy country, and we have not resolutely demanded military and economic sanctions against Japan in Geneva. This is obviously due to the constraints of British gentleman diplomacy, which prevents anti-Japanese diplomacy from being thoroughly implemented. Although this can be forgiven in fact, the main reason why the relations between China and the Soviet Union cannot develop rapidly lies here.

Therefore, in the final analysis, whether the Soviet Union can help us, the answer to this question depends on ourselves. As long as we ourselves can reduce all thoughts of complacency and useless illusions, and show the world our determination to resist Japan to the end, not only will the Soviet Union be able to help us, but all peace-loving and righteous people in the world will also stand on our side.

South China Route to Annam; Yangtze River Route Has Been Connected
