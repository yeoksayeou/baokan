焦土抗戰與治安維持會

— 雍

華北戰事。已由局部之抵禦。進入正面之抗戰廿九軍英勇不撓。為國誓死。南苑之役。高級將吏。相繼陣亡。電訊傳來。薄海悲痛。雖平津情勢。乍起變化。然就全面之立塲觀之。在决戰以前。一城一邑。乍得乍失。初無關於大計者也。

所謂全面立塲。就軍事言之。則為正式之决戰。以卜最後之勝負。事關幃幄。不容贅議。若就國家言之。則蔣委員長。於前此宣言之外。昨日更有嚴切之表示。曰為捍衛國家而犧性到底。日卽使失敗。亦必存與國同盡之决心。决無妥協與屈服之理。換言之。蓋卽所謂焦土抗戰。亦汪精衛氏盧山演說中。滿地尸骸。一片瓦礫。敵人得之。有何用處之意義。為吾舉國人士所宜共守共信之一念。永矢勿諼者。

由此言之。邊論一城一邑之得失。無關於整個戰綫。卽使失敗而至於焦土。亦不當以絲毫物力。貽之敵人。但盡犧性之决心。可獲最後之勝利。乃平津之軍隊。方少移動。而當地僉壬。已有維持會等組織。假名地方之治安。妄冀一時之權利。背叛國家之政策。永貽民族之污點。跡其用心。同於軟敵。可勝誅耶。

原夫維持會等組織。本於北京政府時代。直皖奉直。雞蟲相爭。兩軍相拒於國門。元老坐傷夫塗炭。於是權衡勝負之勢。為定接防之策。終也本是同根。何相煎迫。一則整隊鐃歌以來蒞。一則侵尋款段而退師。蓋以非常之道。濟瓜代之窮。而敵對兩軍。僅屬閱牆。無關民族。是以積習相沿。遂在政權交替間。形成媒介之通例矣。

乃在九一八事變之起。日人初尚不敢遂其鯨吞兼併之計。進而謀諸奸。人奸人甘於卵翼之下。圖得榮利。然在敵勢未固之際。又不敢公然謂他人父。則妄襲維持治安之美名。以為改變民衆心理之初步。俾民衆但求苟安。不問統治。僅顧目前之身家。忘其民族之立塲。形格勢禁。進退自由。成則預傀儡開國之殊功。敗則以地方治安。不涉政治為推諉。初不知當時維持之說。僅為國內兩種政權轉移之緩衝。而絕不能絲毫應用於外族侵凌逼迫之際者也。

至於此次抗戰。國家旣已堅立限度。當局又復屢有闡揚。寗為焦土。决不妥協。乃在不妥協政策之下。一城一邑。乍得乍失之間。卽有妄人起師九一八後瀋陽之故智。仍以身家之顧慮。誘惑民衆。俾為政權轉移之先聲。此其破壞國策。麻醉人民。蓋視倒戈通敵。厥罪尤鉅。蓋國率之受損失於倒戈通敵者。尚僅限於其所屬之一小部份。而此則舉一城一邑之百萬民衆率淪胥其精神。犧牲其物質。用兵之際。我物質一分之减少。卽敵方資源一分之增多。而我百萬民衆之淪胥。則尤與整個民族戰爭。有不可數計之關係。凡我國民。旣抱為國犧牲之决念。對此平津治安維持等會。應有深切之認識。繼以嚴厲之制裁。毋使故都津市。復蹈於瀋陽初步組織之後塵也。

大門上鐵環失竊

— 際雲

××人收買廢鐵　小漢奸貪利活動

上海普通華式房屋的黑漆大門上，左右必有兩個鐵環並排釘着，它的用處，只供開閉時的握手，如果在呌門的時候，也可以代作電鈴，用手按往鐵環向着連擊的鐵板相撞，便發出鐺鐺的聲音，容易驚動屋內人，所得到的效力，比以肉手敵堅木門來得大，雖然對於這個功用和原質上兩方面波甚價值的鐵環，似乎不需要提出來說的必要，却不料這個微小的東西，在最近竟發生了一件窮案，而且關係到國家民族利益問題的事情。

前三天，我們弄堂裏的共十五家石庫門上的鐵環，竟有九家十八個環子在同一個夜裏失了窮。第一個發現這件事的，是一個度夜生活的男子。那天，他在黎明五時歸家，當他用手去担門，摸到鐵環所在地位，突然發現兩個鐵環已經不翼而飛。那時天已大亮，一切都能分辨得清楚，偶一回頭，父見對門和隔壁幾家的也同時不知去向，心裏雖然驚愣，也不說什麼。後來告訴了家裏人，大家也不過跑出來隨意觀察一下，估料大概已作了愉兒的貨品去了，因為價值不高昂，倒也並不在意。

誰知次日，甚餘未遭竊的幾家和偕馬路的十餘家門上的鐵環，也同時失竊，這不免大家的奇怪起來，一只舊織環賣到舊貨店主，不過值二三個銅子，小偷兒對於這種低賤的代價，從未顧問過。也許是近來社會不景氣，影響了偷兒，因此他們不得不委曲點，目標集中到鐵環來。但是別的比較有價值點的和容易偷竊些的東西正多着呢。因為这種環子是用四個長釘牢牢地釘住，偷起來決非容易。發現了可疑之點，非要探它一個究竟不可，況且舊的賣去雖不值錢，但是要裝新的，非二三毛錢不行，如果裝了文偷，這損失實在太大了。於是有人找了管弄堂的來責問，據他說，在上海已在了四十多年，在這里也已經管了十五年的弄堂，這種事情從未發生過，並且生了耳朵也未曾聽見過，俞兒要看相鐵環。大家以為必要想個萬全之策，計議一陣之後，决定由各家公出幾個錢，呌管弄堂的僱了三個人來守夜，果然在昨晚一點鐘模樣，有二個黑衣人，悄悄地進來，用錐子在鑽取鐵環，於是三個守夜人跳出來把他們揪住，逐一呌醒失主來審詢他們。

生長

— 魏克

時光流着，天氣的變，從穿絨衫再要加大衣的季節到祗能披一什襯衫的現在了。但生活哩，似乎是窗外那面一所敎堂的黑屋頂，好像那灰黃色的牆，好像那豬肝色的天水管子，死沉沉的，連些微的變化都沒有。

時常，像一隻無歸處的野狗，拖着管養不良的身子，從這條路蕩到那條路，等到看夠了陌生的現着饑餓病色的面孔，看夠了駛過的無聲的汽車裏豐滿的臉上那滿足的笑，總帶着厭惡的心情，帶着深重的悲哀，我移動着滯重的脚步，回到凌亂的住處。而時間於我是多餘的，又不自覺的靠向臨街的樓窗了。

一顆心不知什麼時候凝固了起來，像是一塊化石；又像是飄蕩在，空中的一片枯葉，什麼地方是停留處呢，這樣的時候……

日子去了不會再來，日子是寶貴的。在以往的日子曾經有過美麗的幻夢：歡樂元憂的日子。但現在却拿嘆息和悲哀的心情送走了每一天。

啊，是自己太懦弱了！

桌上幾張凌亂的紙張塗滿歪斜的小字，那是我煩惱的脚跡呀。可是，把自己訴怨苦的話給誰看呢？有誰是同情他人的不幸的！

作為一個人，應該有好好生活的權利。為什麼要向人乞憐，要受人家的哀憫和施與呢？

心上生長着不平的苗，常倚在窗檻或浪蕩在街路上，倒不是為自己，在我而前經過的，是那麼多的生活在苦痛裏人！

——我們過的是怎樣的生活呀！

這是梅的嘆息。不也是每個人的嘆息嗎？

她的嘆息使我更感覺得痛苦，傳統的觀念支配了一部份的我，我的不能使她生活得舒適點，使我感覺到不安。但當我向四週看得更廣闊，更遼遠以後，我心上的傷口更加擴大了。因此，我也有了安慰梅的藉口了。

——梅，讓我們看得更開些，看速些，把關懷我信自己的心拋到窗外去吧

其實一切我都是多說的，梅並不是倚賴別人的，並不想在這樣的日子裹過舒服的生活。生括的惡浪的沖激，她是一座獨立的岩石，她受過偉大的非在學校裏所能受的敎育。誰會比我更淸楚楚的知道她哩。

但這樣的一個現實，我們被沈重的窒息了。

Scorched Earth Resistance and the Public Security Maintenance Association

— Yong

The war in North China has progressed from local resistance to full-scale resistance. The heroic and indomitable 29th Army has sworn to die for the country. High-ranking officers have successively died in the Battle of Nanyuan. The news has spread, and the whole country is in grief. Although the situation in Peiping and Tianjin has changed suddenly, from the overall perspective, the temporary gain or loss of a city or town before the decisive battle is not related to the overall plan.

The so-called overall perspective, in military terms, is the formal decisive battle to determine the final victory or defeat. This concerns the command tent and cannot be discussed in detail. In terms of the country, Chiang Kai-shek, in addition to his previous declarations, made a more emphatic statement yesterday, saying that he would sacrifice to the end to defend the country, and even if he failed, he would be determined to perish with the country, and there would be no compromise or surrender. In other words, this is the so-called scorched earth resistance, and it is also the meaning of Wang Jingwei's Lushan speech, "a land of corpses and rubble, what use can the enemy get from it?" It is a thought that our people throughout the country should abide by and believe in together, and never forget it.

From this point of view, regardless of the gain or loss of a city or town, it is not related to the entire front line. Even if it fails to the point of scorched earth, we should not leave any material resources to the enemy. Only with the determination to sacrifice can we achieve the final victory. However, just as the troops in Peiping and Tianjin were beginning to move, local officials and gentry had already established organizations such as the Public Security Maintenance Association, falsely claiming to maintain local security, vainly hoping for temporary rights, betraying the policies of the country, and leaving a permanent stain on the nation. Their intentions are the same as appeasing the enemy, and are they not deserving of punishment?

Originally, organizations such as the Public Security Maintenance Association originated in the era of the Peking government. The Zhili-Anhui and Zhili-Feng wars were fought between chickens and insects. The two armies faced each other at the gate of the country. The elder statesmen suffered from the涂炭. Therefore, they weighed the situation of victory and defeat and decided on a strategy for handover. In the end, they were originally from the same root, why force each other so much? The opposing armies were only separated by a wall and were not related to the nation. Therefore, the accumulated habits continued, and a mediation practice was formed during the transfer of power.

They did not know that the so-called maintenance at that time was only a buffer for the transfer of power between two domestic regimes and could not be applied in the slightest to the invasion and coercion of foreign powers.

As for this war of resistance, the country has already firmly established its limits, and the authorities have repeatedly elaborated on it, "宁为焦土，决不妥协" ("Better to be scorched earth than to compromise.") However, under the policy of non-compromise, in the temporary gain and loss of a city or town, there were reckless people who started the old trick of Shenyang after the September 18 Incident, still using the concern for their families to lure the people and become the prelude to the transfer of power. This destruction of national policy and anesthetization of the people is even more serious than turning against the enemy. The losses suffered by the state from turning against the enemy are only limited to a small part of its subordinates, but this is to lead the millions of people in a city or town to ruin their spirit and sacrifice their material resources. During the war, every reduction of one point in our material resources is an increase of one point in the enemy's resources, and the ruin of our millions of people is even more related to the entire national war in countless ways. All our people, having embraced the determination to sacrifice for the country, should have a deep understanding of the Peiping and Tianjin Public Security Maintenance Associations and other organizations and apply strict sanctions so that the former capital and Tianjin will not repeat the mistakes of the initial organization in Shenyang.

Scientific Report from the Arctic Station

▲TASS Moscow dispatch in July: Since the establishment of the Arctic station, the achievements of the scientific work carried out have been summarized by wireless by Che Papanin, Krenkel, Schurshav, and Fedorov, and are roughly as follows:

From May 21 to June 5, the ice floe on which the station was located drifted southwest, with the Greenwich meridian as the standard, at an average speed of four miles per day. From June 5, the ice floe changed to a zigzag flow, varying from east to west, generally between 88 degrees 30 minutes north latitude and 0 to 30 degrees west longitude.

The results of measuring the ocean water revealed that from the ocean surface to a depth of 150 meters, the water temperature was -1.63 to -1.7 degrees Celsius, with low salinity. The temperature then rose sharply, approaching zero degrees at 250 meters, and reaching a maximum of 0.71 to 0.78 degrees Celsius at a depth of 400 meters. Between 275 meters and 600 meters, the water temperature was above zero, with high salinity. Below 750 meters, the water temperature dropped back to below zero, with the ocean floor at -1.23 degrees Celsius. This indicates that the Atlantic current discovered by Nansen in the southern Arctic Ocean actually flows to the polar region, making the area relatively warmer.

At the same time, the results of the exploration proved that Nansen's speculation that there are very few organisms in the center of the Arctic Ocean was incorrect. The nets raised from a depth of 1,000 meters on the ocean floor were rich in various mollusks, echinoderms, jellyfish, and crustaceans. Arctic personnel also measured that the magnetic field there had an average force and no large anomalies. The magnetic meridian deviated 41 degrees west of the Greenwich meridian.

The results of gravity measurements showed a considerable difference from the normal distribution formula of ground gravity. This is quite meaningful and will be further explained after the entire record has been studied in detail.

Municipal Government Announces Amended Housing Tax Regulations

The housing tax regulations of this city have been in effect since their promulgation in the 18th year of the Republic of China (1929), and after so long, in light of current realities, the original provisions all require supplementation or modification. Therefore, the Municipal Government ordered the Finance Bureau to study and revise them article by article, and after submitting them to the Ministry of Finance, they were approved for record. The regulations were officially announced yesterday, with the articles as follows:

Article 1: This city levies housing tax in the urban area in accordance with Article 9 of the City Organization Law, concerning municipal revenue provisions.

Article 2: All houses within this city, except for government agencies and those exempted from tax with the approval of the Municipal Government, are subject to housing tax in accordance with these regulations.

Article 3: Housing tax is borne equally by the owner and the tenant. For self-owned houses occupied by the owner, the entire housing tax shall be paid by the owner.

Article 4: The housing tax of this city will be levied based on improvements in accordance with the law when land tax is levied in accordance with the law.

Article 5: Charitable organizations and other established groups that have not yet reported to the central ministries or the competent authorities of this city for registration shall pay the full tax. However, after registration, tax reduction or exemption may be considered.

Article 6: All municipal schools and registered private schools are exempt from the entire housing tax payable by the school itself. Unregistered private schools shall have their tax reduced by half.

Article 7: Houses owned by the government and leased out are exempt from the owner's portion of the tax. For local public properties, unless the revenue is entirely used for education, charity, or other public welfare purposes, the owner's portion of the tax may be exempted; otherwise, the tax shall still be paid.

Article 8: For all tax-exempt houses, except for those self-produced and self-used that are exempt from the entire housing tax, if they are leased, the owner's portion of the housing tax shall still be levied.

Article 9: For all tax-exempt houses, if the surplus rooms are attached to residential permits or leased for residential or commercial use, the attached or leased portion shall still be taxed.

Article 10: The housing tax rates are stipulated as follows: 12% of the rental amount for residences and 14% of the rental amount for shops.

Article 11: The determination of rent is based on the rent receipt or lease agreement for the most recent two months. However, if the Finance Bureau considers it to be untrue, it may re-evaluate it. For self-owned houses occupied by the owner, the rental value shall be estimated by the Finance Bureau. In case of dispute, it shall be assessed by the Real Estate Valuation Committee.

Article 12: When a house is rented or occupied by the owner, the owner shall report the house address, number of buildings, names of residents, rental amount, or land price and construction costs to the relevant tax collection office of the Finance Bureau for verification and tax collection.

Article 13: When a resident moves out, the housing tax for the current quarter shall be paid in accordance with the method stipulated in Article 17, Paragraph 2. The owner shall report to the relevant tax collection office of the Finance Bureau before the move to cancel the registration in the housing tax register. Residents occupying the top floor should settle the housing tax with the previous tenant and keep it for the Finance Bureau to send personnel to collect. Otherwise, if there is any tax arrears, the current tenant shall be responsible for paying it.

Article 14: If a house owner fails to comply with the procedures stipulated in Articles 12 and 13 of these regulations, resulting in the housing tax being uncollectible, the owner shall be fully responsible for paying the tax. If the owner fails to fulfill the aforementioned responsibilities, the Finance Bureau may deduct the tax from the original rent of the house.

Article 15: Gardens, ball courts, and other places with commercial nature or used for business purposes shall be regarded as houses.

Article 16: Houses that are storefront-style but not used for business shall be taxed as residences. Houses that are not storefront-style but are used for business shall be taxed as shops.

Article 17: Housing tax in the urban area shall be collected quarterly by personnel dispatched by the Finance Bureau's tax collection office to the tenants, and a housing tax receipt shall be issued as proof. Half of the tax payable by the owner shall be paid by the tenant on their behalf and deducted from the rent payable. If a resident moves in within a quarter and has resided for more than half a quarter, the full tax shall be paid. If it is less than half a quarter, the tax shall be reduced by half. If it is less than 15 days, no tax shall be levied. For monthly rents less than three yuan, the tenant's portion of the housing tax shall be waived, except for shops.

Article 18: A housing tax receipt shall be filled out for each address number. If there are multiple households in one address number, a single receipt shall be filled out collectively.

Article 19: Once the tax collection office of the Finance Bureau sends personnel to collect the quarterly housing tax, it shall be paid immediately. If there is any delay or contemptuous behavior, the police station in charge may be consulted to pursue the payment. If necessary, the supply of water and electricity may be suspended. All costs for dismantling and restoring the water and electricity supply shall be borne by the residents.

Article 20: If a tax collector overcharges or makes extra demands, the victim may file a complaint with the Finance Bureau for investigation and punishment in accordance with the law.

Article 21: If there are any matters not covered in these regulations, they may be amended at any time upon consultation.

Article 22: These regulations shall take effect from the date of promulgation by the Municipal Government after being approved by the Ministry of Finance.

Iron Rings on the Door Stolen

— Ji Yun

People Collecting Scrap Iron; Small Traitors Engage in Profiteering

On the black lacquered doors of ordinary Chinese-style houses in Shanghai, there are always two iron rings nailed side by side. Their purpose is only to be used as handles when opening and closing the door. They can also be used as a substitute for an electric doorbell when calling at the door. By holding the iron ring and striking the iron plate it is connected to, a clanging sound is produced, which is more likely to alert the people inside the house than hitting the wooden door with a bare hand. Although it seems unnecessary to mention the iron ring, which has little value in terms of function and material, unexpectedly this small thing has recently become the subject of a strange case, and one that is related to the interests of the nation.

Three days ago, the iron rings on the doors of nine out of fifteen lane houses in our alley were stolen in the same night, totaling eighteen rings. The first person to discover this was a man who lived a nocturnal life. That day, he returned home at five o'clock in the morning. When he reached for the door handle, he suddenly realized that the two iron rings had disappeared. By then, it was already light, and everything could be clearly distinguished. He glanced around and saw that the rings on several houses across the street and next door were also gone. Although surprised, he did not say anything. Later, he told his family, but everyone just came out to observe casually, guessing that they had probably been sold as scrap metal. Because the value was not high, no one really cared.

Who knew that the next day, the iron rings on the doors of the remaining houses that had not been robbed, as well as those on more than ten houses on neighboring streets, were also stolen. This inevitably made everyone feel strange. An old iron ring sold to a junk shop owner is only worth two or three copper coins. Thieves have never bothered with such a cheap price. Perhaps the recent economic downturn has affected the thieves, so they have had to lower their standards and focus on iron rings. However, there are many other things that are more valuable and easier to steal. Because these rings are firmly nailed with four long nails, stealing them is not easy. The suspicious point was discovered, and it was necessary to investigate it thoroughly. Moreover, although old ones are not worth much when sold, it costs two or three dimes to install new ones. If a stolen ring is installed, the loss is really too great. So, someone found the lane manager to question him. According to him, he has been in Shanghai for more than forty years, and has been managing the lane here for fifteen years. This kind of thing has never happened before, and he has never even heard of it. Thieves want to steal iron rings? Everyone thought it necessary to devise a foolproof plan. After a period of deliberation, they decided that each family would contribute some money and ask the lane manager to hire three people to guard the lane at night. Sure enough, at about one o'clock last night, two men in black quietly came in and were using awls to drill out the iron rings. The three guards jumped out and grabbed them, waking up the homeowners one by one to interrogate them.

Their confession was: There is a shop on North Sichuan Road that buys scrap copper and iron. The shop owner is Chinese and offers a high price, specifically for this type of thing, even half an iron key and a rusty nail are accepted, and each pound can be sold for a certain amount of money, which is more than ten times more expensive than at ordinary shops. There is often a person from in his shop. He sometimes asks us in broken and unpleasant Chinese where the iron comes from and what it is used for, etc. Sometimes he also expresses opinions that are both like an expert and like an amateur. According to the shop owner, he is his former master. He used to be a servant in his house, and now his wife is still working as a nanny for his young master. However, one night when we went to deliver goods, we saw that person transport several boxes of old iron out.

After hearing this, we suddenly realized. Haven't the newspapers been reporting on people buying scrap copper and iron these past few days?

From this incident, we can learn a lesson. From now on, we must pay close attention to small metal objects and take strict precautions. Discarding old copper and iron is a daily occurrence in any household. We can also collect them, and we must not be greedy for small gains and fall for the enemy's tricks. Furthermore, those who are willing to be traitors should be severely punished.

——What kind of life are we living!
