「滿家」是日本的生命綫嗎（一）彬

丨謹貢獻於國聯調查團丨

日本自從去年九一八以武襲取我東後。即時世界發放反宣傳說。「滿蒙爲日本的生命綫。」「大和民族的要求爲最少限度的生存權。」「我大和民族如由滿家退却。即大和民族生存權被否認的時候。」（見松岡洋右近著東亞全局之動搖）軍閥政客這樣的呌着。所謂學者們也是這樣的附和着。此一宣傳很是以激起其國民的感情。復足以朦蔽國際人士的耳目。以掩飾其侵略的野心。所以我們有予以嚴正批駁的必要

「滿蒙」是日本的生命綫嗎。還是「滿蒙」自有其偉大的生命。我們要指出此一問題的正確。性第一先從「日本移民地之滿蒙」一點上去體認分析。

現在政友會内閣總理犬養毅氏說。「人口過乘的日本國民。或作爲平和的商人或作爲平和的工業者。或作爲勤勞的農民。不得不向四隣謀融合而移殖。此一事在我民族無寗視其爲最小限度的生存要求爲得當。」所以人口問題在日本人看來。當然爲將來生活的一大問題於是日本軍閥政客們狂呼着「日本人口問題應於「滿蒙」求解决」這種宣傳是何等的動聽。何等的含有誘惑性呵。大多數日本的國民。都被此一宣傳所麻醉了

現在我們得到一正確的答復了。可是並非歐美學者研究的結果。又不是中國人的偏見。乃是出之於日本人之口。還是日本言論界最有權威的學者室伏高信氏研究所得的正確理解。室伏高信氏在去年九一八以後。寫了一本滿蒙論。關於「日本人口問題應於「滿蒙」求解决」一點。駁得最有價値。他說「日本的所謂滿蒙積極政策。果以解决人口問題爲出發點嗎。這點當另作别論。現在先就滿蒙一地是否能解决日本人口問題一點。加以研究。我們先檢查過去的成績。從小村外相即提倡滿蒙移民政策此爲一般人所深知日本經營滿蒙對於此點的注力。當更明白換一句話說日本經營滿蒙的主眼。在增加滿蒙居留的日本人没有妨碍如東洋拓殖會社的設立。田中内閣時滿蒙大移民的設計都本着這意義其結果怎樣呢一九○六年日俄和約以來。曾得到什麽成績。大連爲日本人口增加最多的都市。大正元年日本人三萬四千零四十五人。中國人七萬九千九百七十七人。昭和四年日本人增到九萬零三人。中國人增到二十五萬零九十一人。所謂日本經營在滿洲的唯一都市大連。十八年間只增加了五萬。而中國人則增加了十七萬。可知人口增加。完全爲自然的增加一點。是値得注意的。大連以外的日本人如何分布呢。關東洲（大連除外）一萬五千餘人。滿鐵附屬地九萬三千餘人領事館區域四千餘人。僑居滿洲内地七千餘人。總計二十萬二千五百四十七人。（滿蒙年鑑昭和四年統計）再就在滿日本人口加以解剖我們又可獲得别笪重大的敎訓。官吏和其家族三萬三千餘人。滿鐵社員和其家族三萬四千餘人。滿鐵僱員和其家族四萬六千餘人。共計十一萬四千八百四十七人。（昭和三年大連經濟年史）據此。在滿日本人半數以上爲官吏與滿鐵公司有關係的。雖日本大聲疾呼。以國家力量提倡移民滿蒙。但其結果。日本人移住滿蒙和其自然的增加在二十五年間還不過未滿九萬人。日本人口每年增加八九十萬人從日俄和約以來。已有一千八百萬的增殖。這九萬的移民成績。於日本人口問題能有多大的解决功用呢。」

室伏高信氏把數目字來再估價「日本人口問題應於滿蒙求解决」的不正確。足以給日本軍閥政客們反宣傳以當頭捧喝。可是現在不僅如犬養毅氏所說日本人「向四隣謀融合而移殖。」竟想進而據爲已有稱霸東亞了。這是値得世界人士加以注意的。

Is "Manchuria" Japan's Lifeline? (1) Bin

| Dedicated to the League of Nations Investigation Commission |

Since Japan seized our Northeast by force last year on September 18th, the world has been flooded with propaganda saying, "Manchuria and Mongolia are Japan's lifeline." "The demands of the Yamato race are the minimum for survival." "If our Yamato race retreats from Manchuria, it will be tantamount to denying the survival rights of the Yamato race." (See Yosuke Matsuoka's recent book, "The Shaking of the Overall Situation in East Asia") Military leaders and politicians are shouting this. So-called scholars are also echoing it. This propaganda greatly stirs up the emotions of its people and obscures the eyes and ears of international figures to cover up its aggressive ambitions. Therefore, we must sternly refute it.

Are "Manchuria and Mongolia" Japan's lifeline? Or do "Manchuria and Mongolia" have their own great life? We must point out the correctness of this issue. First, we must recognize and analyze it from the perspective of "Manchuria and Mongolia as Japanese immigrant areas."

Currently, Prime Minister Tsuyoshi Inukai of the Seiyukai Cabinet says, "The overpopulated Japanese people, whether as peaceful merchants, peaceful industrialists, or hardworking farmers, must seek integration with their neighbors and emigrate. Our people must not regard this as the minimum requirement for survival." So, in the eyes of the Japanese, the population problem is naturally a major issue for future life. Then, Japanese military leaders and politicians clamor, "The Japanese population problem should be solved in 'Manchuria and Mongolia'." How appealing is this propaganda! How tempting! Most Japanese people are anesthetized by this propaganda.

Now we have a correct answer. But it is not the result of research by European and American scholars, nor is it the prejudice of the Chinese. It comes from the mouth of a Japanese. It is the correct understanding obtained by Mr. Takashiro Murobuse, the most authoritative scholar in the Japanese journalistic circles. After September 18th last year, Mr. Takashiro Murobuse wrote a book on Manchuria and Mongolia. His rebuttal of the point that "the Japanese population problem should be solved in 'Manchuria and Mongolia'" is the most valuable. He said, "Is Japan's so-called positive policy towards Manchuria and Mongolia really based on solving the population problem? This point should be discussed separately. Now, let's first study whether Manchuria and Mongolia can solve the Japanese population problem. Let's first examine the past achievements. Since Foreign Minister Komura first advocated the policy of immigration to Manchuria and Mongolia, this is well known to the general public. Japan's focus on this point in managing Manchuria and Mongolia is even clearer. In other words, the main focus of Japan's management of Manchuria and Mongolia is to increase the number of Japanese residing in Manchuria and Mongolia without hindrance, such as the establishment of the Oriental Development Company. The grand design for large-scale immigration to Manchuria and Mongolia during the Tanaka Cabinet was based on this meaning. What was the result? What achievements have been made since the Russo-Japanese Peace Treaty of 1906? Dalian is the city with the largest increase in the Japanese population. In the first year of the Taisho era, there were 34,045 Japanese and 79,977 Chinese. In the fourth year of the Showa era, the number of Japanese increased to 90,003 and the number of Chinese increased to 250,091. The so-called only city in Manchuria managed by Japan, Dalian, has only increased by 50,000 Japanese in eighteen years, while the number of Chinese has increased by 170,000. It can be seen that the increase in population is entirely a natural increase, which is worth noting. How are the Japanese distributed outside of Dalian? In Kanto Prefecture (excluding Dalian), there are more than 15,000 people. In the Manchurian Railway Affiliated Area, there are more than 93,000 people. In the consulate area, there are more than 4,000 people. More than 7,000 Japanese live in the interior of Manchuria. The total is 202,547 people. (Statistics from the Manchuria and Mongolia Yearbook, Showa 4th year) Furthermore, by dissecting the Japanese population in Manchuria, we can obtain other major lessons. There are more than 33,000 officials and their families. There are more than 34,000 Manchurian Railway employees and their families. There are more than 46,000 Manchurian Railway employees and their families. The total is 114,847 people. (Dalian Economic Yearbook, Showa 3rd year) Based on this, more than half of the Japanese in Manchuria are officials and related to the Manchurian Railway Company. Although Japan loudly calls for and promotes immigration to Manchuria and Mongolia with national power, the result is that the Japanese who have immigrated to Manchuria and Mongolia and their natural increase have not exceeded 90,000 people in twenty-five years. The Japanese population increases by 800,000 to 900,000 people every year. Since the Russo-Japanese Peace Treaty, there has been an increase of 18 million people. How much can this immigration achievement of 90,000 people solve the Japanese population problem?"

Mr. Takashiro Murobuse's re-evaluation of the incorrectness of "the Japanese population problem should be solved in Manchuria and Mongolia" is enough to give the Japanese military leaders and politicians a head-on blow to their counter-propaganda. However, now, not only as Mr. Tsuyoshi Inukai said, the Japanese "seek integration with their neighbors and emigrate," but they even want to seize it as their own and dominate East Asia. This is worth the attention of people around the world.
