歐戰結束。國際政治之重心巳移至太平洋上。而中國則又恰爲太平洋上風雲叢集之焦點。固早爲世界所一致公認。是故欲維護太平洋上之和平。并避免人類第二次之大屠殺。除尊生中國領土行政主權之完整如九國公約所簽定之明文外。蓋無他道。日出席九國會議之幣原代亦曾宣稱。「日政府之立塲。對於中國無人可以否認其自行統治之神聖權利。亦無人可以阻撓其造成偉大之國家命運。」今則盟誓在耳。口血猶新而竟一佔東北。再犯淞滬。舉凡盟約之尊嚴與國際信義。皆悍然不顧是則與其謂日迫害中國。毌寗謂爲迫害世界。與其謂日爲中國之敵或美國之敵。毌寗謂爲世界之公敵。華盛頓政府爲欲維護九國公約之尊嚴。曾放棄其戰艦製造之領袖地位並打銷甘姆菲列濱繼續設防之計劃。故九國公約復與海軍公約息息相關。破壞九國公約。即無異於破壞海軍公約。即無異於脅迫美國放。棄其現在之海軍政策。擴充其海軍以準備太平洋上之角逐。而此情勢。必又將立即引起倫敦之注意。重新掀起國際間海軍競爭之黑浪。與太平洋上不太平之怒潮。

勢之所趨。不得不爾故日人此次在我上海挑起戰禍。實爲世界第二次大戰之發端然則日本之罪惡。不更爲世界人士所灼見耶。自史汀生此函發表心後。在國際間顯然巳引起良好之反應。英泰晤士報孟却斯德報。都著論力表贊同法報稱爲美國遠東政策偉大之宣言北京。而講壇導報則更直指爲對芳澤脆弱靜議之痛快的反駁。視此國際之輿論色調巳漸趨顯明。使日軍終不覺悟。則華盛頓府對日。必將作進一步之有力表示遠東情勢重大之轉捩。爲期勢必不遠吾人於此蓋可預爲斷言。我國於國家際間一切盟約。向爲忠實之擁護者。於史氏斯函維護世界和平之熱忱。自表深重之敬意獨是吾人須認淸際此風雲幻變之秋世界各國莫不各有其立塲。而此立塲又莫不各視其本身利害而决定。換言之。即美有美之立塲。我有我之立塲。如依賴史氏斯函之所表示。而視爲拯我危困之靈符者不爲短視。即爲依賴成性。美對於制裁日人之暴行。縱有從行動上積極表示之决心。但至少亦必有一二年長時問之準備。之補充。與論之製造。其尤重要者大選舉實爲一大關鍵。故美即欲有所表示。亦决不在目前。在國際政局未有變動之前吾人猶處於孤軍奮鬥之難境。除竭盡全力。拼命苦鬥。以謀打破當前之難關而外决無他道亦决無其他倖存之理

Reading Stimsons' Letter to Borah (Part Two) 1

The European War has ended. The center of international politics has already shifted to the Pacific Ocean. And China just happens to be the focus of the gathering storm in the Pacific. This has long been unanimously recognized by the world. Therefore, to maintain peace in the Pacific and avoid a second great massacre of humanity, there is no other way except to respect the territorial and administrative integrity of China as clearly stipulated in the Nine-Power Treaty.幣原喜重郎, the Japanese representative at the Nine-Power Conference, once declared: "The Japanese government's position is that no one can deny China's sacred right to self-rule, nor can anyone hinder its creation of a great national destiny." Now, the oaths are still ringing in our ears, and the blood is barely dry, yet they occupy the Northeast and then invade Shanghai. They disregard the dignity of treaties and international faith. Therefore, rather than saying that Japan is persecuting China, it is better to say that it is persecuting the world. Rather than saying that Japan is the enemy of China or the enemy of America, it is better to say that it is the common enemy of the world. To uphold the dignity of the Nine-Power Treaty, the Washington government abandoned its leading position in battleship construction and scrapped the plan to continue fortifying Guam and the Philippines. Therefore, the Nine-Power Treaty is closely related to the Naval Treaty. Destroying the Nine-Power Treaty is tantamount to destroying the Naval Treaty, which is tantamount to coercing the United States to abandon its current naval policy and expand its navy in preparation for a contest in the Pacific. This situation will inevitably immediately attract the attention of London and rekindle the black wave of international naval competition and the turbulent tides of unrest in the Pacific.

The trend of events is inevitable. Therefore, the Japanese provoked war in Shanghai this time, which is actually the beginning of the Second World War. Then, isn't Japan's crime even more clearly seen by the world? Since the publication of Stimson's letter, it has clearly aroused a good reaction internationally. The British Times and the Manchester Guardian have both published articles strongly expressing their support. The French newspapers hailed it as a great declaration of American Far East policy. The Beijing Morning Post directly pointed out that it was a delightful rebuttal to the fragile 静议 (peaceful discussions) of 芳泽 (芳泽谦吉, Japanese Foreign Minister). It is evident that the international public opinion is gradually becoming clear. If the Japanese army does not wake up in the end, then Washington will definitely make further forceful representations to Japan. The turning point of the major Far East situation is bound to be not far away. We can foresee this. Our country has always been a faithful supporter of all international treaties. We express our deep respect for Mr. Stimson's enthusiasm for maintaining world peace. The only thing we must recognize is that in this autumn of rapidly changing situations, all countries in the world have their own positions, and these positions are all determined by their own interests. It plans to organize a self-defense force of 100 people in the city and suburbs to strengthen security.

Bai Jizhi, director of the Joint Military and Police Inspection Office, recently issued a notice with County Magistrate Shen, stating that the war is currently fierce and this county is close to Shanghai. In order to consolidate the rear and maintain security, starting from the date of the notice (25th), pedestrians will be prohibited from traveling after 11:00 p.m. and food and lodging shops should close before the appointed time.

On the 25th, the last train from Shanghai to Hangzhou passed through Xinlonghua Station. Suddenly, enemy aircraft were discovered in the sky, flying along the railway line, watching for an opportunity to drop bombs on the locomotive. The passengers were greatly alarmed and got off the train to hide in the bamboo forests and fields, hoping to avoid danger. As expected, two enemy bombs were dropped, but fortunately, they did not hit the target. One fell into the mud and did not explode, and the other fell on a pile of firewood and coffins, causing coffins to fly around. Later, the enemy aircraft returned to Shanghai, and the train escaped danger and continued to drive. However, the passengers were all frightened. Yesterday morning (26th) at around 6:00 a.m., another twelve enemy aircraft flew west along the Huangpu River. When passing through Songjiang, they flew very high. According to news from Hangzhou, Qiaosi Airport was bombed by enemy aircraft, so the enemy aircraft passing over Songjiang must have been going to Hangzhou.
