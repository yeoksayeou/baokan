租界應保持其中立性　彬

上海惨變自上月二十八晚爆發以迄今日屈指已近一旬，此旬日。間吾人但日聞炮聲隆隆。機聲軋軋。但日見屍體狼藉遍地。難民擁擠於途。東亞第一之大巿塲已渝爲極可恐怖之塲麵所。凡屬關心遠東和平之人士。應都瞭然於此一惨變性質之嚴重。凡足以弭止此嚴重禍變之有效方法。應立即採取施行。同時更基於公理與正義之立塲上。對此一惨變作公道之判斷。

然而就事態之現狀而觀吾人對於負有維持租界治安責任。及可能維持和平重責之公共租界當局尚有數點。爲吾人所大惑不解者。敢提以質之公共租界當局。

第一。當惨變積極醖釀行將爆發之初。租界當局。分配防務。特劃蘇州河以北北江西路以東。東至楊樹浦沿淞滬鐵道線一帶。歸日本軍隊警備。夫泥城橋以北及楊樹浦一帶。原爲美租界於一八六三年與英租界合并。成立公共

租界。與日人固無與也。今劃此極易與我軍接觸衝突之地帶爲其警備區。不無予日人以軍事上便利之。嫌此吾人所不解者。一

第二。公共租界。固保有絶對的中立性質者也。自一八四三年成立以至目前。凡八十九年。中經中法之戰。清日之戰。與一九一四年至一九一八年之世

界大戰。公共租界及。上海港。都被公認爲絶對的中立。性不加侵犯。而此次日人竟不惜破壞其中立性。憑藉租界爲根据地。進以襲擊我軍。退以防守。城狐社鼠。有所恃而無畏。虹口一帶。概成戰區。同時日軍負責警備虹口一帶。其自身已成爲惨變之禍首。是否仍能負荷警備之責。亦已大明。乃租界當。局迄今尚未採取緊急有效之處置。以弭止禍變之擴大。此爲吾人所不解者二。

第三。虹口以至楊樹浦一帶租界内居民。對於租界當局。平時負有納税及其他種種義務。則租界當局。對於界内居民。應負有保障生命財產安全之義務。亦毫無可疑。今虹口一帶。已渝爲戰區。在日人暴行下。居民之生命財產。已毫無保障可言居民遭受無辜之屠戮而無可告訴似租界當局於其所應負荷之義務。尚未能盡。此爲吾人所不解者三。

總之。上海惨變。非單純之上海局部問題實即太平洋以至全世界之居問題。日人之横暴無信。藐視列國。勃勃野心。尤爲全世界人士所共見。据前日電訊。日駐美大使出淵謂美聯社代表曰。「野村海軍中將現赴上海。與美海軍總司令台納少將作球。戲兩領袖作球戲。時兩軍亦必作球戲也。」其趾高氣揚目中無人之神態。蓋活躍可掬。故全世界主張公道之人士。亟應起而共同制裁。而負有維持租界治安之當局。尤不可不恪守嚴格之中立者也。

The Concessions Should Maintain Their Neutrality – Bin

The Shanghai Incident has erupted since the evening of the 28th of last month, and almost ten days have passed. During these days, we have only heard the booming of cannons and the rattling of engines. We have only seen corpses scattered all over the ground and refugees crowding the roads. The largest market in East Asia has been transformed into an extremely terrifying scene. All those who care about peace in the Far East should be fully aware of the seriousness of this tragic incident. All effective methods to stop this serious disaster should be adopted and implemented immediately. At the same time, based on the standpoint of public reason and justice, a fair judgment should be made on this tragic incident.

However, based on the current state of affairs, we have several points regarding the Public Concession authorities, who are responsible for maintaining the security of the concessions and who may bear the important responsibility of maintaining peace, that we find greatly puzzling. We dare to raise them to question the Public Concession authorities.

First. When the tragic incident was actively brewing and about to erupt, the Concession authorities, in allocating defense duties, specifically designated the area north of the Suzhou River, east of North Jiangxi Road, and east to the Yangshupu area along the Songhu Railway line, to be guarded by the Japanese army. The area north of the Mud City Bridge and the Yangshupu area were originally merged by the American Concession with the British Concession in 1863 to establish the Public

Concession. It has nothing to do with the Japanese. Now, designating this area, which is extremely prone to contact and conflict with our army, as its security zone is not without giving the Japanese military convenience. This is what we do not understand. One.

Second. The Public Concession inherently maintains absolute neutrality. Since its establishment in 1843 until now, 89 years have passed. It has gone through the Sino-French War, the Sino-Japanese War, and the World

War from 1914 to 1918. The Public Concession and the Port of Shanghai have both been recognized as absolutely neutral and inviolable. However, this time, the Japanese have disregarded its neutrality and used the Concession as a base to attack our army and retreat for defense. Like city foxes and shrine rats, they have something to rely on and are fearless. The Hongkou area has generally become a war zone. At the same time, the Japanese army is responsible for guarding the Hongkou area. The Japanese army itself has become the main culprit of this tragic incident. It is unclear if it can still shoulder the responsibility of guarding the area. The Concession authorities have not yet taken emergency and effective measures to stop the expansion of the disaster. This is what we do not understand. Two.

Third. Residents in the concession area from Hongkou to Yangshupu bear the obligations of paying taxes and various other obligations to the Concession authorities in normal times. Then, the Concession authorities should bear the obligation to ensure the safety of the lives and property of the residents in the area, which is also beyond doubt. Now, the Hongkou area has become a war zone. Under the brutality of the Japanese, the lives and property of the residents are no longer guaranteed. Residents are being slaughtered innocently without recourse. It seems that the Concession authorities have not fulfilled their obligations. This is what we do not understand. Three.

In summary. The Shanghai Incident is not simply a local issue in Shanghai, but a global issue concerning the Pacific Ocean and even the whole world. The Japanese are brutal, untrustworthy, and disregard other countries. Their ambitious desires are especially evident to people all over the world. According to a recent telegram, the Japanese ambassador to the United States, Debuchi, told the Associated Press representative: "Vice Admiral Nomura is now going to Shanghai to play golf with Rear Admiral Taussig, the commander of the U.S. Navy. When the two leaders play golf, the two armies will also play golf." His arrogant and supercilious attitude is indeed vivid. Therefore, people all over the world who uphold justice should rise up and impose joint sanctions. The authorities responsible for maintaining the security of the concession must strictly adhere to neutrality.

Victory in Aerial Combat

Japanese Pilots Burned to Death

In fact, during peacetime, Japan and other countries stationed a certain number of troops in Shanghai.
