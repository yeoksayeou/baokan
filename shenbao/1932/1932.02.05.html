從上海恐怖說到遠東和平

彬

自從去年九一八東北禍變以後有人即預料上海有如一座火藥庫有立時爆發的可能果然這火藥庫現在是已經爆發了從上月二十八以至目前這極其危險的燃燒與爆裂正朝着非常恐怖的悲境推并進并擴大很顯然的被燃燒着的不僅是首當其衝的中國就整個世界而觀察却正是爆發太平洋上戰禍的一根引綫換言之即是破壞遠東和平的制命的一刀其危機實較之去年九一八所爆發的東北禍變尤爲嚴重

爆發這火藥庫的砲手無疑義的自然是日本帝國主義者所以日本帝國主義者與其說是中國的仇人母寗說是世界的公敵世界各國爲消弭迫害全人類的戰禍而在國際聨盟軍縮會議諸集會中焦慮討論而日本帝國主、義者的砲艦與大隊的海陸空軍正自東京向着上海進行殘酷的屠殺公然毫無顧　向全世界挑釁又是何等的大膽呵

當　日帝國主義者這種大膽的殘暴行爲不是偶然的它是由於整個世界的　景氣及其本身特殊的嚴重危機所激成先天的澆薄與後天的虧僓是日本帝國主義者生相特别兇殘的重要原因處於日暮途窮的恐慌悲境而加緊對弱小民族的侵凌與掠奪以企圖延長其殘喘那是必然的結果然而日帝國主義者向中國加緊侵略果能减輕其恐慌延長其日瀕危亡的壽命嗎事實上恐爲不可能的幻夢日言論界的權威者室伏高信氏於日本强佔東省之後著有满蒙論一書曾說「日本軍費大正元年爲一億地元最近爲二億三四千萬元再加以海軍計每年爲五億元積極政策直接的結果

第一爲日本在满洲須有較今日更多的軍隊第二爲朝鮮須有較今日更多的軍隊第三爲日本須特别對陸軍加以充實」我們且不言對華積極侵略將日益加緊列强間之尖稅對立使日帝國主義者恢復其繁榮的幻夢永難實現就是如上室伏所說鉅大軍費額的增加已足使日本人民負担日益加重日益貧困化市塲日益縮小日益走上破落之途了

爲不景氣所苦悶的不只是日本一國整個世界都是這樣以戰爭解决矛盾第一次世界便是這樣爆發的這一次當然也不能例外太平洋上刻正波詭雲譎大有山雨欲來風满樓的形勢遠東的和平已瀕於十分險惡的悲境如果日本臨到最後關頭再不覺悟則可怖的事件爆發即在目前至多不過進行的路程多經過幾次紆迴曲折罷了

Battle Situation in Wusong Yesterday

The naval and air forces launched a fierce attack on the Wusong Fort the day before, failing and being sunk by our fort with one Japanese destroyer, three warships damaged, and one plane shot down. Yesterday, the Japanese Naval Headquarters sent a large number of warships to surround and attack the Wusong Fort again, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Our army responded with all its strength, and two Japanese warships were injured, and they never crossed the Lei Chi pool one step. According to the investigation by this newspaper, the details are recorded below.

Enemy Warships Surround and Attack, Our Newspaper's Reporter Stationed in Song reported to the museum last night that around 9:00 AM yesterday, there were 13 enemy warships surrounding the fort again. The fortress commander, Zhen Quan, immediately supervised the officers and soldiers to fire back. The two sides exchanged fire fiercely. Until around 11:00 AM, two enemy warships were attacked by our Pudong Longwang Temple Fort, severely damaged, and lost their fighting ability. They retreated from the fort's firing line with the remaining undamaged ships. At 0:30 noon, the enemy warships launched a large-scale attack again, with more intense artillery fire than before. Enemy Marines also landed separately in the north of Baoshan City and at the Yang Wharf in front of the Baotai Railway Station, covered by enemy warship artillery fire, hoping to intercept the fort from the land. After the fortress garrison troops joined forces with the 19th Route Army, they repelled the enemy. At the same time, the Laoshusha Guanghua Oil Depot across the river from Zhang Huabang in Wusong was landed by enemy soldiers, setting fire to disrupt the rear. Around 2:00 PM, five enemy warships and eight or nine enemy planes sailed to Songkou and joined the battle. For a time, the artillery fire on the fort side was continuous, shaking the sky. Enemy soldiers landed on the coast in several places and were repelled one after another. A ceasefire was only reached around 5:00 PM. The residents of Songding have already moved away completely.

▲Annihilation of Japanese Army Starting at 9:00 AM yesterday morning, 13 Japanese warships launched a large-scale attack on our fort. Our army fought bravely, and the two sides fought for several hours. Suddenly, our fort became silent. The Japanese warships continued to fire, but there was no response from our side. The Japanese urgently sent reconnaissance planes to fly near the fort to spy, also believing that our army had completely retreated and the fort was destroyed. Therefore, they reported by radio. As a result, the Japanese Marines landed one after another. When they approached our fort, our ambush soldiers rose up and fired fiercely, and swept with machine guns. More than a thousand Japanese Marines were annihilated, and none survived.

After a fierce battle until around 3:00 PM, our side stopped returning fire, pretending not to resist. The enemy ships thought that our side had retreated, so they sent more than a thousand Marines to land, intending to directly capture the fort. Our defending soldiers quietly waited for the enemy army to approach the fort, and then used machine guns and mortars to sweep. The enemy army could not escape and was killed by our army for more than a thousand people, and almost no one survived. ▲Going to Song for Command The Japanese naval and air forces have been attacking our Wusong Fort for days, and our army has fought bravely and fiercely. The fortress area is still firmly guarded by our army.

▲Deng Expresses Wusong Fortress Commander Deng Zhenquan expressed to all sectors yesterday that this time the violent Japanese army hoped to invade Zhabei. Japan Rejects Item Five

Reply Delivered to Envoys of Three Nations

Tokyo, Japan's reply to the five proposals from the powers, was delivered by Foreign Minister Yoshizawa to the British, American, and French ambassadors at 6 p.m. tonight. The document accepts the first item: both sides cease violent actions; rejects the second item: no further preparations for hostile actions; and completely accepts the third and fourth items: the withdrawal of Chinese and Japanese combatants from all points of contact within Shanghai, and the establishment of a neutral zone, guarded by neutral countries, to protect the International Settlement, the arrangements for which will be made by local consular authorities. The document stated that if the Japanese army is certain that the Chinese will immediately and completely cease threatening and disruptive actions, then the Japanese army will also agree to cease hostile actions. Due to the past Chinese threats and the severity of the current situation, the Japanese government feels it cannot completely abandon mobilization or preparations for hostility. The Japanese government considers the Manchurian issue to be completely unrelated to the Shanghai incident. The Manchurian issue will be handled in accordance with the resolution passed by the League of Nations Administrative Council on December 10, and it will not accept the assistance of neutral observers to resolve various issues concerning Manchuria, which is the established policy of the Japanese government.
