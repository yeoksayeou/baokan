「滿蒙」是日本的生命綫嗎（二彬·）

丨謹貢獻於國聯調查團丨

爲了自身的利益。日本的軍閥財閥與政客一致主張對滿蒙積極政策。而以「日本人口問題應於滿蒙求解决」之狂呼。欺騙國際人士。麻醉彼國人民以掩飾其侵略的野。心此一反宣傳。在日本學者室伏高信氏無情的揭露之下。其眞相當已爲世界人士所灼見。室伏氏所謂「此種積極論不過僅爲滿鐵總裁·政黨政治家·滿洲浪人·及一部分企業家之要求」。誠可謂爲「一針見血。」

再從另一方面觀察近二十五年來日本移居「滿蒙」的人口。除官更與滿鐵從業人員而外／爲數僅九萬餘人。這不僅已指出了「滿蒙」决不是日本的生命綫。反之另一方面的事實却明白地證明滿蒙確爲中國的生命線。　　室伏氏在同書中曾說「山東移民最近七年間。（一九二二年至一九二九年）共有五百萬定住者近二百五十萬。我們試考量此正確的事實。則滿蒙非日本人口問題的解决地實山東人口問題的解决地。」二十五年間日本移殖「滿蒙」的人口。不過九萬人。而七年間山東移殖「滿蒙」的人口。已達二百五十萬。「滿蒙」究是誰的生命綫呢。這不已經是無須辯論的問題了嗎。

其實室伏氏所指陳的事實。還只是極小的一部分。依據我國方面統計。（見東北年鑑）十七年關内各省移殖「滿蒙」的人口。總數達一百零八萬九千人。十六年與十八年。年都在一百萬以上。從此種鉅大的數字而觀。即可想見我關内各省人口之稠密。與生活之艱難。那末滿蒙之確爲我生命線。不尤其成爲鐵一般的事實。絶對的不可否認嗎。

「滿蒙」爲我國的領土。我國握有不可侵犯的主權。就令退一步說滿蒙爲日本的生命線。但究依據於何種理由。得以强力掠取呢。英屬澳大利亞。美國西部各州。尤其是加州不是都較中國爲地曠人衡嗎。但對於日本的移民。都加以嚴厲的限制然則日本除以强權解釋公理而外。所謂「滿蒙爲日本生命線」的呼喊還有存立的餘地嗎。再退一步言就令滿蒙誠爲日本的生命線。但如上述事實。亦足證明其確爲我國的生命線日本又究依據於何種理由。可以奪取我國人民的生存權。專以維持日本人的生存呢這又是人道與正義所允許的嗎。

最近滿蒙在日人一手包辦之下。已經成立了所謂滿洲僞國。在僞國還未成立之前。對於主人地位的關内移民。即很迅速的加以禁止。抱有獨占的野

心。這即是日本的軍閥與財閥們實際剝奪中國人民的生存權。破壞中國領土的完整。全世界愛護公理與人道的人們。其注意此種陰謀的手段呵。

Is "Manchuria and Mongolia" Japan's Lifeline? (By Erbin)

丨Respectfully Contributed to the League of Nations Inquiry Commission丨

For their own interests, Japan's militarists, zaibatsu, and politicians are unanimously advocating an aggressive policy towards Manchuria and Mongolia, clamoring that "Japan's population problem should be resolved in Manchuria and Mongolia." They deceive international figures and anesthetize their own people to conceal their aggressive ambitions. This propaganda has been ruthlessly exposed by Japanese scholar Takashin Murofushi, and its true nature has already been clearly seen by the world. Murofushi stated, "This aggressive theory is merely the demand of the South Manchuria Railway President, party politicians, Manchurian adventurers, and a segment of entrepreneurs." This can truly be described as "hitting the nail on the head."

Furthermore, observing the population of Japanese who have immigrated to "Manchuria and Mongolia" in the past twenty-five years, excluding government officials and South Manchuria Railway employees, the number is only over 90,000. This not only indicates that "Manchuria and Mongolia" are definitely not Japan's lifeline, but conversely, the facts clearly demonstrate that Manchuria and Mongolia are indeed China's lifeline. Murofushi stated in the same book, "In the past seven years (1922 to 1929), Shandong immigrants had approximately 5 million permanent residents, with nearly 2.5 million settlers. If we consider these accurate facts, then Manchuria and Mongolia are not the solution to Japan's population problem, but the solution to Shandong's population problem." Over twenty-five years, the Japanese population that has immigrated to "Manchuria and Mongolia" is no more than 90,000, while in seven years, the Shandong population that has immigrated to "Manchuria and Mongolia" has reached 2.5 million. Whose lifeline are "Manchuria and Mongolia" after all? Isn't this a question that requires no debate?

In fact, the facts pointed out by Murofushi are only a very small part. According to statistics from our country (see the Northeast Yearbook), in the seventeenth year [of the Republic of China, i.e., 1928], the total number of people who immigrated to "Manchuria and Mongolia" from the provinces within the Great Wall reached 1,089,000. In the sixteenth and eighteenth years [1927 and 1929], the annual number was over one million. From such a large figure, it can be imagined how dense the population and how difficult life is in our provinces within the Great Wall. So, isn't the fact that Manchuria and Mongolia are indeed our lifeline even more ironclad and absolutely undeniable?

"Manchuria and Mongolia" are our country's territory, and our country holds inviolable sovereignty. Even if we take a step back and say that Manchuria and Mongolia are Japan's lifeline, based on what reason can they forcibly seize them? Aren't British Australia and the western states of the United States, especially California, more sparsely populated than China? But they all impose strict restrictions on Japanese immigrants. Then, besides using power to interpret justice, is there any room for the so-called "Manchuria and Mongolia are Japan's lifeline" cry to exist? Taking another step back, even if Manchuria and Mongolia are truly Japan's lifeline, as the facts above demonstrate, it is sufficient to prove that they are indeed our country's lifeline. Based on what reason can Japan seize our people's right to survival and exclusively maintain the survival of the Japanese people? Is this permitted by humanity and justice?

Recently, under Japan's complete control, the so-called puppet state of Manchuria has been established. Before the puppet state was established, the immigration of people from within the Great Wall, who were in the position of the masters of that land, was quickly prohibited, harboring ambitions of exclusive control. This is precisely the Japanese militarists and zaibatsu actually depriving the Chinese people of their right to survival and destroying the integrity of Chinese territory. May all people in the world who cherish justice and humanity pay attention to these insidious means.

The public security authorities, fearing that traitors might be mixed in with the inland, have specially joined forces with the merchant corps, army, defense corps, and water police to conduct thorough searches, which have uncovered several cases: (1) The 6th Company of the 2nd Battalion of the 1st Regiment of the Garrison in Wuxi arrested 11 people, including Wang Ziyu from Funing, at the No. 168 railway bridge, and also found an ordinary grenade along the way, which was handed over to the camp headquarters for investigation. (2) The 4th Branch of the Public Security Bureau outside the West Gate seized a barge, capturing five suspected traitors: Xu Linquan, Xu Jinsheng, Xu Desheng, Zhang Zhesheng, and Xu Xiangyang, and found a "Great Japan Meiji 8th Year" silver coin, several Guangxu coins, and a Hong Kong silver coin on their persons, which were transferred by the Public Security Bureau to the county government for interrogation. (3) The 3rd Branch of the Public Security Bureau arrested three people, including Chen Ronggen, at the No. 6 workshop in Yangyaowan, South Gate, and seized a large number of fur coats and gold ornaments, as well as Guangxu and Guangwu coins, which were sent to the Public Security Bureau for interrogation. (4) The 1st Branch of the Public Security Bureau arrested five vagrants, who were transferred by the County Public Security Bureau to the Garrison Camp Headquarters for investigation. It is also heard that the vagrants were involved in gang-raping boatwomen from Jiangbei. (5) The 10th Detachment of the Merchant Corps seized a barge in the Jiangjian River and found opium, red pills, banknotes, and other items, which were transferred to the County Court for interrogation.

Gaoyou Correspondence: Li Wei, the director of the 1st Branch of the County Public Security Bureau, arrested a middle-aged man with strange clothing in Zhongshikou yesterday (the 12th) and immediately took him to the bureau for preliminary interrogation. According to his confession, his name is Zhou Qingzhen, also known as Zhou Dagouzi, a native of this county, and he is the chief of staff of the National Salvation Volunteer Army. The army has more than 100,000 men in the area bordering Jiangsu and Shandong provinces and will soon pass through Gaoyou to the south to participate in the war in Shanghai. He then confessed that he was in fact bought by the Japanese side. Director Li considered the case to be very serious and immediately transferred it to the county government. Judge Jiang opened a court session for interrogation. According to his confession, last year he participated in the robbery of Chen Liangguang's home in the 5th General, 6th Li, along with others, and absconded with the loot. Judge Jiang immediately ordered the co-conspirator, Xia Hengyi, to be brought to court for questioning, and his confession was the same. After the interrogation, he ordered that the two criminals be detained first, pending further investigation and processing.
