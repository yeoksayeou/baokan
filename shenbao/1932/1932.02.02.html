國民必須注意三事

在今日急迫情勢之下。我全國人民。都應一致認定我們當前唯一之敵人爲日本帝國主義者。尤其應以一致之堅决意志。一致之整齊步伍。集四萬萬人爲一人與我們當前之敵人拚死血戰一切黨派。都應放棄其政見。與黨或派之利益。集合於反日帝國主義爭取民族自由之革命旗幟下來。此時絶不容各持異見。各懷鬼胎。以互相率掣。終至於誤國。并以亡國。朝鮮之亡。不僅是由於日帝國主義者之侵凌。而其最大之原因則尤爲强敵壓境而内部黨爭猶紛擾不息。凡此前轍。皆我殷鑑。所以我們目前唯有凝結一片散沙爲堅牢之水門汀。而後可以言與頑强之勁敵相搏戰。否則適爲自殺。適爲自誤誤國。其結果必至永陷我民族於萬刦不復之深淵。

次之。吾人認定目前我民族唯一之敵人爲日本。故除日本以外世界各國皆爲我之友邦。吾人應以友善之態度。避免一切足以危及友邦人士之舉動。并保障其安全。吾人尤其希望世界各國基於公理及正義之立。塲對日帝國主義者之暴行。作公正之發言。吾人認爲日帝國主義者數十年來之野心與暴行。不僅爲我國之敵人。亦即全世界全人類之公敵。中國人民今已不得已而肩荷此反抗强暴爭取光明之重任。全世界愛好和平與公理之人士。都應予以鉅大之助力。

又次。我國人一方以全力援助軍隊之工。作一方尤應以全力維持後方之秩序鎭定惶懼之人心蓋後方秩序不安。人心不定即足以影響前方之工作因是之故。我後方之人民。應竭力維護地方之安全。金融界應盡力維護金融。工商界應盡力維護工商業。官廳各機關。應盡力設法。使地方人民鎭靜。免致秩序混亂。我人民尤應家喩房曉。勿爲無稽謠言所惑。人心苟稍搖動即足使不逞之徒。乘機騷動。影響前方工作甚大。此最爲必須注意之事。

Three Things the People Must Pay Attention To

In today's urgent situation, all of our nation's people should unanimously recognize that our only current enemy is Japanese imperialism. In particular, we should, with a united and resolute will, and with coordinated and orderly steps, gather the 400 million people as one to fight our current enemy to the death. All parties should abandon their political views and the interests of their parties or factions, and gather under the revolutionary banner of resisting Japanese imperialism and striving for national freedom. At this time, it is absolutely unacceptable to hold different opinions and harbor ulterior motives, leading to mutual obstruction and ultimately harming the country and leading to its demise. The fall of Korea was not only due to the invasion of Japanese imperialism, but its greatest cause was the internal party strife that continued unabated despite the pressure of a strong enemy. All these past examples are a profound lesson for us. Therefore, we must now consolidate a pile of loose sand into a solid cement before we can talk about fighting the stubborn and powerful enemy. Otherwise, it would be suicide, self-deception, and harming the country, and the result would be to plunge our nation into an abyss of eternal doom.

Secondly, we recognize that our nation's only current enemy is Japan. Therefore, all countries in the world except Japan are our friendly nations. We should maintain a friendly attitude and avoid any actions that could endanger people from friendly nations, and protect their safety. In particular, we hope that all countries in the world, based on the principles of justice and righteousness, will make fair statements regarding the atrocities of Japanese imperialism. We believe that the ambitions and atrocities of Japanese imperialism over the past few decades are not only the enemy of our country, but also the common enemy of the entire world and all mankind. The Chinese people have now been forced to shoulder the heavy responsibility of resisting tyranny and striving for light. All peace-loving and just people in the world should provide great assistance.

Furthermore, while our people are doing their best to assist the army, they should also do their best to maintain order in the rear and calm the hearts of the fearful. If the rear is in disorder and people's hearts are unstable, it will be enough to affect the work at the front. Therefore, our people in the rear should do their best to maintain local security, the financial community should do their best to maintain finance, and the industrial and commercial sectors should do their best to maintain industry and commerce. Government offices and agencies should do their best to find ways to calm the local people and prevent disorder. Our people should especially be made aware in every household not to be misled by unfounded rumors. This is the most important thing to pay attention to.
