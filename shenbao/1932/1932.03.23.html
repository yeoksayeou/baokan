目前我國政治上之危機。彬· 1

自去年七月間洪水之泛濫。與九一八東北禍變突發之震撼。處於凋敝之進程中之中國。乃益陷於黑暗如漆之狀態。至是政府亦已深感焦頭爛額。寖且慮無力打破目前之艱局。而昔日之軍閥官僚政客·以爲國民巳忘其禍國殃民之罪戾。乃欲企圖死灰之復燃。從國難重重局面之下。各攫取一臠。各分得一杯之羹故目前我國死臨到空前之嚴重時期。

中國本爲文化發逹最早之國家近數世纪以來。文化停滯。迄無進步。大多數人民之政治水平綫。尤其低落所謂「不在其位。不謀其政。」「食於人者治人。食人者治於人」之思想。直至目前。猶支配我國大多數人民之頭腦。對於政治抱不聞不問之態度。今日不知明日。今日亦不問明日。但望「天生聖人。爲民之則」但望在位者爲「好人。」爲「賢人。」此種好人政之希望。事實上已爲自袁世凱以來之「個人主義」「軍閥政治」所澈底撃破。

民國十五年。國民黨以有主義。有政綱。有組織。有立塲。有光榮之革命歴史之革政黨。乃代之而興。軍閥政治。乃暫趨於銷沉而代之以政黨政治。政治上亦曾一度放射空前未有之曙光。

迨國民黨革命勢力逹到黄河流河域投機之舊軍閥官僚政客。亦讀遺嘱。談主義。一變而爲國民黨之忠實黨員。如煕洽張景惠之流。竟一躍而爲黨部之指導委員。國民黨中少數領袖。但求彼輩爲已所用。但求表面上得以底定全國。亦一律收容。在此時期。國民當黨在國內政治上旣無復强有力之勁敵於是個人主義乃重新抬頭。頻年以來。一般政治乃漸與人民之利益。背道而馳。軍閥政治與官僚政治。亦緣而復興民困水火。外患遂乘隙而入。故自去年九一八禍變突作。危機乃日益增大。寢至於不可收拾。

令日吾人對於政府。固不忍作過苛之責難。然而在事實上。則目前國難。與未來之出路。事人處此嚴重時機。究將如何乎。其能仍抱「在農言在工言工在商言商」之態度。對於有切膚關係之政治不加過問。坐視舊軍閥官僚政客捲土重來。或其他一般以個人利益爲出發點之政治組織。作種種之活動。以共向現政府要求利益均霑。機會均等乎。令日國內則人慾横流國外則强敵環伺。吾人已臨到最後之時機。周詳考慮。急起救。不復再機不可失。國人其善自處自擇。

The Current Political Crisis in Our Nation. Bin. 1

Since the devastating floods of last July and the sudden shocking outbreak of the September 18th Northeast Incident, China, already in a state of decline, has been plunged further into darkness. The government is deeply overwhelmed and fears it lacks the strength to break through the current difficult situation. Former warlords, bureaucrats, and politicians, believing that the people have forgotten their crimes of harming the nation and the people, are attempting to revive their power. From this chaotic national crisis, they each seek to grab a piece, to get a share of the spoils. Therefore, our country is currently facing an unprecedentedly serious situation.

China was originally the first country to achieve cultural development. However, in recent centuries, cultural stagnation has occurred, with no progress made. The political awareness of the majority of the people is particularly low. The ideas of "not concerning oneself with affairs outside one's position" and "those who are fed by others govern others; those who feed others are governed by others" still dominate the minds of most of our people. They adopt an attitude of indifference towards politics, not knowing what tomorrow will bring and not even asking about it. They only hope for a "sage born by heaven to be a model for the people," only hoping that those in power will be "good people" and "virtuous people." This hope for governance by good people has been thoroughly shattered by the "individualism" and "warlord politics" since Yuan Shikai.

In the fifteenth year of the Republic [1926], the Kuomintang, as a revolutionary party with principles, a political program, an organization, a stance, and a glorious revolutionary history, rose to replace them. Warlord politics temporarily declined, replaced by party politics. A dawn, unprecedented in its brightness, once shone on politics.

When the revolutionary forces of the Kuomintang reached the Yellow River region, opportunistic old warlords, bureaucrats, and politicians also read the will and talked about principles. They transformed themselves into loyal members of the Kuomintang. People like Xi Qia and Zhang Jinghui even became guiding members of the party headquarters.
