介紹蘇聯的兒童敎育彬

四四兒童節又和白駒過隙一般的過去了然而目前我國成千成萬的兒童所遭受的深重苦痛與兒童節的眞實意義是否曾在聰明的父兄和兒童敎育家的腦底激起一些微波。引起一點點作用呢。在此。我們特再簡略的介紹一點關於蘇聯兒童敎育的事情。以供國人比較研究和參考。蘇聯敎育的目的。是造成當作社會有用的一員。當作健全而有勞動能力。與滲透着社會的意識組織化的訓練。認識而且理解自己在自然及社會中的位置。明白其四週的現實。有能力建設社會主義社會的「社會人。」至於敎育方針則從初等敎育以至高等敎育。都由自然。勞動。社會的根本敎材之三部分。出發把國家建設的新分野上的全部文化革命都委之於敎育。這是蘇聯的新興敎育之特點許多人説新興的蘇俄。如今正是一個世界的敎育實騐室。確實的。在蘇聯人類的物質與精神的一切活動。都正在作一次新的實騐敎育不過是其正在實騐中的一端。

兒童敎育。在蘇聯尤其是被重視。因爲他們知道負荷建設社會主義之重責者。正是這一輩純潔活未受舊時代之薰陶的兒童所以與其説蘇聯兒童敎育的目的在於從今後不使蘇聯再有一代的兒童甚至一個兒童在無知與愚昩中長大母寗謂其在於培植此萬千幼苗都成爲撑持社會主義社

會的幹材。

關於兒童敎育。在學齡前。即喚起對於自然的興味。引入勞作的敎育。同時有組織地學習交際。以爲兒童社會性之發展與訓練。其方法。有託兒所。以保育勞動者及紅軍兵士的乳兒及嬰兒有幼穉園。以敎育較大的孩提。有所謂夏期的「兒童廣塲。」與冬期的「兒童廣塲。」以爲兒童夏期與冬期的運動保嬰機關。這種學校的工作。不僅是替代了兒童的父母的保育責任。使其得以自由工作。并且實爲父母社會敎育的中心。

至於身體有缺陷及知能發逹程度低的兒童。也爲之設有肓唖敎育機關與低能兒敎充機關再如一般無保護人之浮浪兒。及在敎育不適當的家庭中的兒。童又設有對少年之社會的保護敎育機關。如幼年館如少年館。未成年館等課以社會的敎育與勞動。

總之。蘇聯是國家實際担任了全國兒童的保姆的責任。他們不僅是使每一個兒童得活潑地生存而且使其以普遍的享受敎育。他們努力於促進兒童之生理學的合法的發育。維持并增進其健康同時更使其在和現實文化密切關聯之下。用其手與腦在生活的各方面活潑地學習實踐人類敎育的第一個階段。全蘇聯的兒童便是在這様的環境之下生長着少年的蘇聯也就在這様的環境之下生長着。

我國的兒童生活與兒童敎育的方法是怎様呢。在此我們希望我們的政府。以及父兄們。與兒童敎育家們作一次比較的考察。一次　密的深思。

忍痛開市

— 一臧

海上各界。自從罷市以來。直到四月一日。始忍痛復業。在這兩個多月內。百業停頓。地方糜爛。不論直接間接所受的損失。幾乎難以數字計算。在戰區內的居民。已以毀家蕩產。而在戰區外的商店。爲了顧客的死亡逃避。歇業倒閉。已民沒有蹤跡可尋。所放的賬欵。一時難以收集。即使他們還存在。可是所有產業已補摧毀無遺。大半已沒有能力。否則前賬不清。新貨難進。雖開市。恐怕不易繼續營業。兼之交通尚未恢復。而外埠多不明滬地真相。風聲謠傳。都是裏足不前，雖然忍痛復業前途。未許樂觀。

回憶日本東京當大地震以後。情況的悲慘。殆與現在相同。然而他們因爲得了政府的援助。和各方的體諒。加以商業道德交易信用等關系。能以互助的精神。打破危急的難關。所以不數年。就能恢復市場的繁榮。這次我們爲了禦外侮而遭犧牲其與天災的性質大不相同。所以各方尤須共體時艱。互相調解。切不可對於受害的商店或顧客勒逼過甚。自相傾軋。反爲強暴者所貽笑。

現在地方各界。已傾其全力。籌商善後的辦法。銀錢業也爲顧念大局。設法通融。縱不能一時恢復舊觀。但也可以維持市面。茍延殘喘。惟有一點還請各界特別注意者。我們受這加空前的浩刦。各人腦袋中當然有深刻不忘的印象。聽說最近我們心痛的貨物。行將源源而來。倘若再不稍覺悟。那末眞糟到極點了。

記石瑛修建普濟道院

— 甫髯

吳縣閶門外白馬澗鎮西二里餘。有名賀九嶺者。一小山也。上有普濟道院。風景絕佳。頗宜修養。惟院屋破幾。久鮮人居。廼去處十二月間。忽煥然一新。往探之。始知爲石瑛所修。蓋瑛自浙主席張難先去逼供後。即隨而辭去建設廳長。有感於世局艱難。人心詭譎。故悠然而隱棲於彼。一切修建。皆出自己囊。院內共六人。二爲司灑掃炊爨啟閉者。亦其鄉人。一爲老道。終日伴瑛談論者。瑛自亦道裝。二爲其子。於前月始來。由其父親自敎讀。當其子之來也。所有行李。令鄉婦肩之上山。給以四百文。實則對折已足。瑛知之大怒。責以混賬。蓋惡其故示闊綽也。於是可知其嚴矣。瑛來時。所帶行李無我我。除臥被外。惟書籍一箱而已。不失書生本色。待人亦頗和氣。本鎮自去冬始設郵箱。惟地處僻野。信札極少。日不過三四封而已。自瑛來後。遽然增多。每日往返。彼個人必占址數件。郵票以是而得暢銷也。一笑。

變愛救國，原是現在最新鮮，最時髦的一個名詞。只要化去三四塊大洋，就可以完成我倆的終身大事，建築我倆的愛情墳墓。天空軋軋的飛機，看牠則甚丨崑山太倉等處的砲聲，聽牠則甚！吳淞江灣閘北等處的慘狀，不干我事，管牠則甚！稱心如意，天作之合，我願天下有情人多成眷屬。

但是這一類的廣告，若照我分晰起來，其中至少右分兩種。一種是愛情熱烈，借此公開的。一種是經濟壓迫，藉此節省的。惟在國難方殷的時候，這種愛情，是否可以永保。若云經濟壓迫，則結婚之後，在一方又須加上一重負擔，經濟豈不是愈加困難了麽？總之，靑年時代的要素，不在婚姻，婚姻的確不是終身的大事。求學是終身大事，職業是終身大事。欲完成國民的最大志願，最要使命，是終身大事。

試問國難方殷，是你們訂婚的時期麽？國難方殷，是你們結婚的時期麽？古人有「國幾不國無以家爲。」今人有「國難方殷結婚啓事。」相映成趣，無怪有人説古今人不相及呢！

— 孟復

火藥（Ｐｏｗｄｅｒ）在古時就有了，我國也使用得很早到了近代，科學昌明，火藥的用途益廣，不論在工業上，軍事上部佔了重要的位置，尤其是在軍事上，因爲現代的戰爭，差不多是以火藥爲中心的，假使世界上的火藥完全没有了，人類問永久和平的希望也許能夠實現·現在我國外侮日迫，自衛的戰爭恐怕是無法避免了，所以特地把火藥提出，作一個简單的研究·

火藥究竟是什麽？火藥者，受衝擊力或熱力，即起劇烈之變化，發生高熱及多量氣體之不定化合物或混合物之名稱也依組成之性質，可分爲混合火藥與化合火藥二大類，略述在下面：

（Ａ）混合火藥

（一）黑色火藥丨此藥可算是火藥中的鼻祖，由硝石（ＫＮＯｍ）硫黃（Ｓｕｌｐｈｕｒ）

和木炭三物混合而成·三物分量之比，最普通大槪是硝石百分之七十五，硫黃百分之十，木炭百分之十五·以木炭爲燃燒之主體，硝石含養氣甚多，即由此支助燃燒，硫黃具可燃性和粘結性，可助木炭之燃燒及粘結木炭與硝石，使不分離而增加爆發作用·自從無烟火藥發明後，幾乎要淘汰了此藥，不過現在仍用作礦山的低力爆發藥及緩燃的導火綫等·

（二）褐色火藥丨此藥係黑色火藥之變體，亦爲木炭、硝石、硫黃、混合而成·不過木炭、硝石、硫黄、混合而成日褐色·爆發力田較黑色火藥高，從前皆用作發射藥，不過無烟火藥出世，也漸漸絕跡了·

（Ｂ）化合火藥

（一）硝化棉系烟火藥丨取淸凈之棉花，浸於硝酸（ＮｉｔｒｉｃＡｃｉｄＨＮＯ３）

與硫酸（ＳｕｌｐｈｕｒｉｃＡｃｉｄＨ２ＳＯ４）之混合液中，二者相化合，即生一種硝化纖維素·但硝化纖維素不止一種，依硝化度之高下而分，大槪可得八種，凡硝化物不溶解於酒精及以脱（Ｅｔｈｅｒ）之混合液中者，謂之强棉火藥·能溶解者·謂之弱棉火藥·取適量之强棉及弱棉火藥之藥棉混合·依法即可製成藥粒，現今槍今

砲子彈中皆用之·因此藥發火正確，而且爆力甚大，又燃燒後，全體火藥，皆成氣體，不餘殘渣，如此可使槍砲身筒不致污穢，實在是發射火藥中最良好的·

（二）硝化甘油系無烟火藥丨加純粹甘油於硝酸及硫酸之混合液中，過半小時，二者相化合而成硝化甘油，取適量之棉火藥，加於硝化甘油中，依法亦可製成此種無烟火藥，性質與硝化棉火藥大抵相同·且爆發力較强，就發射力上講，似爲有利，惟爆發之温度過高，砲身易遭炸燬，此一點實爲不及硝化棉火藥·製造價尚低廉，因爆發力强大，故開礦等工業上所用之爆發藥，大槪以此藥作主要成分·

最第要講火藥的威力，大槪火藥每一基羅（Ｋｉｌｏｇｒａｍｍ），爆發時所發生的氣體，比火藥原有的體積，要大萬餘倍·十六吋口徑之大砲彈，（重約一千餘斤）自發動至砲口，僅需百分之四秒·此尙是低級火藥，高級强烈火藥之威力·眞是不堪設想啊！

火藥的種類，尙有多種，現在不過舉其主要的幾種而已·詳細的火藥製造法亦從略，因爲在大衆科學上的東西，應該大衆化一點，上面所講，不過要使讀者們對於火藥有一些認識罷了·

Introducing Soviet Children's Education

The Double Fourth Children's Day has passed like a fleeting white horse. However, have the profound sufferings endured by tens of thousands of children in our country and the true meaning of Children's Day stirred up any ripples in the minds of our intelligent fathers, brothers, and educators? Has it triggered even a little action? Here, we will briefly introduce some aspects of Soviet children's education for our countrymen to compare, study, and reference. The purpose of Soviet education is to cultivate individuals who are useful members of society, healthy and capable of labor, and imbued with social consciousness and organized training, who recognize and understand their place in nature and society, understand the reality around them, and have the ability to build a socialist society. As for the educational policy, from primary education to higher education, it is based on the three fundamental components of natural science, labor, and society, entrusting the entire cultural revolution in the new fields of national construction to education. This is the characteristic of the emerging education in the Soviet Union. Many people say that the emerging Soviet Russia is now a world's education laboratory. Indeed, in the Soviet Union, all human material and spiritual activities are undergoing a new experiment, and education is just one aspect of this experiment.

Children's education is particularly valued in the Soviet Union. Because they know that the burden of building socialism rests on this generation of pure, lively children who have not been influenced by the old era. Therefore, rather than saying that the purpose of Soviet children's education is to prevent any child in the Soviet Union from growing up in ignorance and ignorance in the future, Mother Ning said that it is to cultivate these thousands of young seedlings into pillars of socialist society.

Regarding children's education, before school age, it is to arouse interest in nature, introduce labor education, and systematically learn social skills to develop and train children's sociality. The methods include nurseries to care for the infants and babies of laborers and Red Army soldiers, kindergartens to educate older children, and so-called summer "children's playgrounds" and winter "children's playgrounds" as institutions for children's summer and winter sports and childcare. The work of these schools not only replaces the childcare responsibilities of children's parents, allowing them to work freely, but is also actually the center of social education for parents.

As for children with physical defects and low cognitive development, special education institutions for the blind and deaf and institutions for the mentally retarded are also established. Furthermore, for general unprotected street children and children in families with inadequate education, there are also social protection and education institutions for young people, such as juvenile halls and adolescent halls, which provide social education and labor.

In short, the Soviet Union, as a state, actually assumes the responsibility of being the nanny for all the children in the country. They not only ensure that every child survives vigorously but also enables them to enjoy universal education. They strive to promote the physiological and legal development of children and maintain and improve their health. At the same time, they enable them to learn and practice actively in all aspects of life with their hands and brains, closely linked to real-world culture, which is the first stage of human education. The children of the entire Soviet Union are growing up in such an environment, and the young Soviet Union is also growing up in such an environment.

What are the methods of children's life and education in our country? Here, we hope that our government, fathers, brothers, and educators will conduct a comparative study and a careful reflection.

Popular Science

Gunpowder

— Meng Fu

Gunpowder has existed since ancient times, and our country has used it very early. In modern times, with the flourishing of science, the uses of gunpowder have become more extensive, occupying an important position in both industry and military affairs. Especially in military affairs, modern warfare is almost centered on gunpowder. If all the gunpowder in the world were completely gone, perhaps the hope for permanent peace among mankind could be realized. Now, our country is facing increasing foreign insults, and a war of self-defense is probably unavoidable, so I specifically bring up gunpowder to conduct a simple study.

What exactly is gunpowder? Gunpowder is the name for unstable compounds or mixtures that undergo drastic changes when subjected to impact or heat, generating high heat and a large amount of gas. Based on the nature of its composition, it can be divided into two major categories: mixed gunpowder and compound gunpowder, briefly described below:

(A) Mixed Gunpowder

(1) Black Gunpowder: This gunpowder can be considered the ancestor of gunpowder, made by mixing saltpeter (KNOm), sulfur (Sulfur), and charcoal. The ratio of the three substances is most commonly about 75% saltpeter, 10% sulfur, and 15% charcoal. Charcoal is the main component for combustion, saltpeter contains a lot of oxygen, which supports combustion. Sulfur has combustible and cohesive properties, which can help the charcoal burn and bind the charcoal and saltpeter together to prevent them from separating and increase the explosive effect. Since the invention of smokeless powder, this powder has almost been eliminated, but it is still used as a low-power blasting agent in mines and as a slow-burning fuse.

(2) Brown Gunpowder: This powder is a variant of black powder, also made by mixing charcoal, saltpeter, and sulfur. However, the mixture is brown. The explosive power is higher than black powder. In the past, it was all used as propellant, but with the advent of smokeless powder, it has gradually disappeared.

(B) Compound Gunpowder

(1) Nitrocellulose Smokeless Powder: Take clean cotton and immerse it in a mixture of nitric acid (Nitric Acid HNO3) and sulfuric acid (Sulfuric Acid H2SO4). The two combine to produce a type of nitrocellulose. However, there is more than one type of nitrocellulose. Depending on the degree of nitration, about eight types can be obtained. Any nitrate that is insoluble in a mixture of alcohol and ether is called strong cotton gunpowder. Those that can be dissolved are called weak cotton gunpowder. Take an appropriate amount of strong and weak cotton gunpowder and mix it to make powder grains. This is used in current guns and cannons. Because this powder ignites accurately and has a very large explosive force, and after burning, the entire powder becomes gas, leaving no residue, which prevents the gun barrel from becoming dirty, it is truly the best propellant powder.

(2) Nitroglycerin Smokeless Powder: Add pure glycerin to a mixture of nitric acid and sulfuric acid. After half an hour, the two combine to form nitroglycerin. Take an appropriate amount of cotton powder and add it to nitroglycerin. This type of smokeless powder can also be made in accordance with the law. Its properties are generally the same as nitrocellulose powder, and its explosive force is stronger. From the perspective of launching power, it seems to be beneficial, but the explosion temperature is too high, and the gun barrel is easily blown up. This is inferior to nitrocellulose powder. The manufacturing price is still low. Because the explosive power is strong, most of the explosives used in mining and other industries use this powder as the main ingredient.

Most importantly, the power of gunpowder, roughly speaking, the gas generated by the explosion of each kilogram of gunpowder is more than ten thousand times larger than the original volume of the gunpowder. A 16-inch cannon shell (weighing about 1,000 catties) only takes four hundredths of a second from launch to muzzle. This is still low-grade gunpowder. The power of high-grade strong gunpowder is truly unimaginable!

There are many types of gunpowder, but now I am only listing a few of the main ones. The detailed gunpowder manufacturing method is also omitted because things in popular science should be more popular. The above is just to give readers some knowledge of gunpowder.
