政治黑暗與敎育破產彬 1

昨評發表後。記者復廻環沉思。深感以學風替下。敎育破產。獨責敎育界。未免失之太偏。且或亦非敎育界人士所願聞。蓋敎育之破產。其最大根因。則仍由於政治之黑暗也。

十六年國民黨誓師北伐。全國學生。多奮起響應。其於革命盡力至多。而黨所致力之靑年運動。則更以喚起靑年努力從事革命爲口號。故經歷民十六十七革命高潮而後。一般學生對於「讀書問題。」已深感政治而不上軌道。社會而長此停滯。「讀書」實毫無所爲有重新估量其價値之必要。而寖以傾向政治减低其讀書之興趣。迨國民黨旣底定全國。對於靑年之自由思想。即開始施以裁制。敎育黨化。訓練亦以黨之意志爲轉移。黨之所是者。必皆是之。黨之所非者。亦必皆非之。彼生機甫萌之靑年。乃忽遭受無情之桎梏。一綫之光明。忽又重返於黑暗。於是而强者不恤走險。弱者則悲觀銷沉。流入奢靡驕縱之途。以自戕其生命之力。此所以致今日之惡果者一也。

不僅此也。自國民黨藉民衆之力以取得政權。復藉政治之力量以壓抑民衆勢力之伸張。於是而軍閥政治與官僚政治乃重新抬頭。割據勢成。内戰時作。官僚當政。貪汚專橫。但知利己。不恤人民。於是而政成黑暗。而軍閥則又但知養兵。以增强其爭雄長之實力。政客但知中飽。以富裕其快慾念之私囊。國家財政。旣已費去百分之八十以養兵。而政費則又再經官僚之中飽。敎育旣不爲彼輩所措意。更敎費更可恣意拖欠。邇年以來。各國立學校以經費之無着。已顏於一致關門之狀態。從事敎育者生計之艱苦。更匪言所能宣。呼籲請求。全國一致。而敎育經費之長年欠發也如故。從事敎育者亦以生活艱難。不能安心於其所事。而軍閥政客又常藉口「反動。」橫肆摧殘。此的以致今日之惡果者二也。

邇年以來。敎育界已爲官僚政治所侵入。敎育行政。旣無固定計劃。敎職則更視安揷私人之位置。敎育界中。派别分歧。壁壘森儼。門户各别。競奪位置。怪象百出。多數學校之風潮。大都有不肖黨人與政客爲其主動。所謂黨化敎育。恰爲黨人操縱敎。育於是而賢者灰心。不肖之輩乃更得以恣意。此致今日之惡果者二也。

吾人從可知政治而不淸明。凡百皆無整飭之望。而今日學風之敗壞。與敎育之破壞。固不能獨以此重責敎育界。凡此惡果。皆以黑暗政治種其惡因。

雖然。敎育界仍不能遂謂爲無責也。敎育之目的。在於改變現實。推進社會。在於敎導靑年與現實奮鬥。劍造未來之新天地。是則處於今日黑暗政治之下。敎育界人士爲自救計爲救靑年計爲救敎育計。急應起而與政治上之惡勢力奮鬥。與社會惡環境奮鬥。以敎育改造政治與社會培。養民族未來健全之萌芽。此乃眞爲敎育界人士當前應負之重責。記者謹以無限之熱忱。祝望眞能愛護敎育。眞肯致力敎育事業諸君切實負起。

Political Darkness and Educational Bankruptcy 1

After yesterday's commentary was published, the reporter once again pondered deeply. I deeply feel that attributing the decline in academic ethos and the bankruptcy of education solely to the education sector is somewhat too one-sided. Moreover, it may not be what people in the education sector want to hear. The greatest root cause of the bankruptcy of education still lies in the darkness of politics.

In the sixteenth year, when the Kuomintang vowed to launch the Northern Expedition, students across the country rose up in response. They devoted themselves to the revolution to the greatest extent. The youth movement the Party was committed to even took awakening the youth to strive for revolution as its slogan. Therefore, after experiencing the revolutionary climax of the sixteenth and seventeenth years of the Republic, the general student body deeply felt that with politics not on the right track and society stagnating for a long time, "studying" was actually useless and it was necessary to re-evaluate its value. They gradually tended toward politics and reduced their interest in studying. When the Kuomintang had stabilized the entire country, it began to impose restrictions on the free thinking of young people. Education was party-ized, and training was also shifted according to the will of the Party. What the Party affirmed, must be affirmed. What the Party denied, must be denied. The young people, whose vitality was just sprouting, suddenly suffered ruthless shackles. A glimmer of light suddenly returned to darkness. Thus, the strong risked danger, while the weak became pessimistic and depressed, drifting into extravagance and indulgence, using their life force to destroy themselves. This is one of the reasons for today's evil consequences.

Moreover, since the Kuomintang seized power by relying on the strength of the masses, it used political power to suppress the expansion of mass forces. Thus, warlord politics and bureaucratic politics re-emerged. Separatism formed, and civil wars occurred from time to time. Bureaucrats in power were corrupt and tyrannical. They only knew self-interest and did not care about the people. Thus, politics became dark. The warlords only knew how to raise troops to increase their strength in the struggle for hegemony. Politicians only knew how to enrich themselves in order to satisfy their desires. Eighty percent of the national finances were spent on raising troops, and government expenses were further embezzled by bureaucrats. Education was not valued by these people, and education funds could be arbitrarily delayed. In recent years, national schools at all levels have faced the state of unanimous closure due to a lack of funding. The hardship of the livelihoods of those engaged in education is beyond description. Appeals and requests were made unanimously across the country, but the perennial arrears of education funds remained the same. Those engaged in education were unable to concentrate on their work due to difficult living conditions. Warlords and politicians often used the excuse of "reactionaryism" to wantonly destroy things. This is the second reason for today's evil consequences.

In recent years, the education sector has been invaded by bureaucratic politics. Educational administration has no fixed plans, and teaching positions are regarded as a place to place personal cronies. Within the education sector, factions are divided, and barriers are strict. Each faction has its own territory, competing for positions, and strange phenomena abound. The majority of school unrests are mostly initiated by unscrupulous Party members and politicians. The so-called party-ized education is exactly the Party manipulating education. Thus, the virtuous become discouraged, and the unscrupulous are able to act arbitrarily. This is the third reason for today's evil consequences.

We can see from this that if politics is not clear, there is no hope for rectification in anything. The failure of today's academic ethos and the destruction of education cannot solely blame the education sector. All these evil consequences are due to the dark politics planting evil seeds.

However, the education sector cannot be said to be without responsibility. The purpose of education is to change reality, promote society, and teach young people to fight against reality and create a new world of the future. Therefore, under today's dark politics, education sector personnel should urgently rise up and fight against the evil forces in politics and against the evil social environment in order to save themselves, save the youth, and save education. Use education to transform politics and society, and cultivate the healthy seeds of the nation's future. This is truly the important responsibility that education sector personnel should bear at present. The reporter sincerely wishes that all those who truly love education and are truly willing to devote themselves to the education cause will earnestly take on this responsibility.
