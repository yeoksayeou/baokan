日本往那裏去

爲着想打破其嚴重而且深刻的不景氣。并延續挽救其已陷於沒落悲境的命運。日本的財閥及其爪牙丨丨軍閥。决然更加緊其對於弱小民族之掠奪。採取强硬的砲艦政策。由緩和的經濟侵略。進而爲直接的武力壓迫。這是去年九一八東北事件以至此次上海禍變爆發之主因。亦即日本的財閥與軍閥的唯一企圖。

去年九一八日本突然暴力發動。强佔瀋陽。東北二十餘萬大軍。在張學良不抵抗命令之下。望風引退。使東北半壁河山。不崇朝而盡變顏色。這種事實。不僅使日本的財閥軍閥們拍掌歡呼慶幸其目的之達到。抑更趾高氣揚。得意忘形。視中國如無人之境。所以這次敢於大膽的又在上海揭開戰幕。其始料何嘗不如鹽澤向領團誇大的言。「四小時内可將上海華軍全部肅淸。」然而自上月二十八至現在。日每戰必敗北。以我軍英勇抗戰。其志終不得逞。這不僅日本的財閥軍閥意料所不及。應亦爲世界各國人士所認爲不可解之奇蹟。

這是日本財閥軍閥們的一個當頭敎訓這敎訓。不僅是暴露了日本的軍隊無作戰經騐。氣餒無鬥志。與此次日本師出無名。以及中華民族尚未容世界藐視。尚足以反抗强暴。其尤重要者。爲顯示其對華積極侵略之絕對不能打破不景氣。反足以增加其不景氣之深刻。并增重其沒落的危機。據前日本報載鹽澤談話說。「這次戰事是中國應受的敎訓。」究竟是誰應受的敎訓呢。

因爲對華積極侵略自去年九月十八以來日本對華貿易很顯然的一落千丈。至最近上海禍變發生。更陷於全部停頓。這種現象不僅是日本財閥軍閥們的一個鉅大創傷簡直是一下致命的打擊。其結果將使日本產業界更加凋零。失業的羣衆更加激增金融界更加速其動搖。換　之。即其崩潰之命運更日益迫近眉睫。

日本財閥軍閥們出兵的口實。爲「保僑」爲「制止中國的反日運動。」然而出兵的結果怎樣呢日前日本大批婦孺自上海返國時的凄凉與悲慘的景象。該足以說明「保僑」的結果了罷。幾月來我國反日運動之日益普遍與擴大。該足以說明「制止反日運動」的結果了罷。而且因爲動員大軍。軍費激增。更使預算呈露鉅大之破綻。因爲鉅額士兵之犧牲。更使人民厭惡政府之窮兵黷武。而加重其政治上之危機。今日電訊。前任藏相井上氏突遭暗殺。這顯然的爲經濟問題之故。軍閥與財閥之勾結。將自此破裂了。又據近日消息。日東京帝國與慶應兩大學敎授學生及市民。作廣大的示威運動。其口號爲「反對日帝國主義進攻支那。」神户工人及兵士遊行示威。大阪海員罷工。其口號爲「不到支那去。」「不打中國的兄弟。」「反對日帝國主義者進攻支那。」這口號不是已顯示了東亞霸王的日本。已在急劇的動蕩變化中。其轉變的前途。不可以隱若望見了嗎。

對華積極政策。將能收得什麽效力呢。日本的財閥軍閥們。此時應該深悔當初一念之差。致鑄成今日九洲之大錯了罷日本將走向何處去呢。日本的財閥軍閥們。又將走向何處去呢。

Japanese soldiers exert all their strength.

Zhenjiang City is Calm

The Provincial Government Has Not Yet Moved to Yangzhou

However, Financial Institutions Have Been Suspended

Zhenjiang Correspondence: On the day following the Shanghai incident, train tickets from Zhenjiang only went as far as Suzhou, and traffic to Shanghai was interrupted immediately. The sound was close, and the morale of the soldiers was even more courageous. Zhenjiang banks experienced a slight run three days ago. The provincial government and the Chamber of Commerce held a meeting to discuss relief measures. Because it was inconvenient to transfer funds from Shanghai, all bank notes were temporarily not redeemed. The market remained in common use as usual. In the past two days, it has been fairly stable. However, small shops refuse to give change in single notes or silver dollars for five-yuan notes. Uninformed people even lower prices. If a method for redeeming them is not devised, it may be difficult to maintain the original price in the long run. Copper coins are only exchanged for 2,400 cash per yuan. Banks closed their doors on the second day because all the banks in Shanghai had closed. Today (the fourth day), all the money shops also closed one after another. Government work is as usual. There was initially a rumor that they were moving to Jurong, but the government denied it. Guangdong Deploys Border Defenses to Aid Shanghai

Air Force Transfers Half of its Military Aircraft to Shanghai to Assist in Combat

The Navy, Army, and Air Force Unite Against Japan, Fully Armed and Prepared

Guangzhou Special Dispatch: Since the outbreak of the Shanghai war, the people of the Baiyue region have been filled with grief and indignation. The military authorities have also unanimously decided to jointly face the national crisis. Telegrams to the central government and to aid the 19th Route Army from all the navy, army, and air force generals were telegraphed on the evening of the 29th. At 10:00 AM on the 30th, Chen Jitang convened a national defense conference with the generals of the First Army Group. The army commanders, division commanders, chief officers, and the Air Force commander-in-chief also attended. Concrete measures were planned regarding consolidating the Southwest border defense and assisting the frontline soldiers. Due to military secrets, they will not be released for the time being. However, Guangdong Province is an important gateway to South China and must be defended in advance. Because Japanese warships in Shantou fired indiscriminately at pedestrians on the shore for no reason on the 29th, in addition to ordering the 2nd Independent Division of Zhang Ruigui to send troops to strictly guard the coastal defense along the mountains, Humen in Guangzhou should also be properly deployed. Therefore, Chen Jitang personally went to Humen on the 31st to inspect the defenses there, addressing the Humen Garrison Guard Brigade of Chen Hanguang and the 4th Division of Zhang Da, and inspected the fortresses.
