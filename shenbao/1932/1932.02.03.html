應付嚴重局勢之辦法彬 1

自從上月二十八晚禍變爆發以來。上海正向着極端危險與恐怖的前途推進着。此數日中槍彈悲鳴。血肉横飛。飛機鄉的擊大火的焚燒。東亞第一大市塲的上海。以及居住於上海的三百萬無辜的人民。已整個困陷於破壞與恐怖襲擊的悲境了。

不可避免的禍殃。今旣已毫不遲疑地展開於我們之面前。吾人則惟有毅然肩承以英勇的精神。起而奮鬥。暴日旣已置我們於死地。從死境中求得生路。端在於我們自身艱苦之爭鬥。故目前我們無所用其驚惶。尤無所用其恐怖。要當以沉毅的精神。鎭靜的態度。一致的步伍。有計劃有組織的行動。奮起和强敵搏戰。

第一。我們認爲商界應即日開市。以安定人心。因爲罷市三天。已足以表示我們一致抗日悲壯激越的情緒。但假如長此罷衣。却很足以搖動人心。增加後方的恐怖。並減低一般人奮鬥的力量。正爲日人所期望欣愉。爲安定後方秩序計。所以必須即日開市。照常營業。

第二。我們認爲銀行亦應即日開市。以安定金融在此目前人心惶惶之際。銀行開市。如恐發生危險。則應請租界當局負責防維。如恐提欵者過多。發生動搖。則銀行界儘可妥自籌。謀嚴加限制。且聞銀行界本已準備充足。自可無慮。同時社會對此種擾亂金融。間接帮助敵人的行動。也可予以正義之制裁。不必多所顧慮。銀行業一日不開市。社會金融。即一日不得穩定。而其影響所及。全國各地。亦必引起恐慌。因此銀行業必須早日開市。

第三。我們認爲一切力量。必須集中。必須在有計劃有組織之行動下。分途進行。如慰勞前敵將士。如救濟戰地同胞等工作。都應於協助地方政府與租界當局之。外更由各公法團體產生一最高權力機關。通盤籌劃。集中力量。共同進行慰勞物品。必適合於前敵將士之需要。救濟災民。不必收容一處。亦可籌妥善之方法。送往外埠。其他如調劑糧食。救濟失業。維持交通。安定秩序。都應協力共籌。力聚則收效大。散則收效微。此點我們必須認淸。

總之。此次日人用心在於造成上海的大恐怖。我們必須以鎭靜的態度。有計劃有組織的行動。維持社會秩序。消弭此種恐怖。使敵志無由得逞。

Methods to Cope with the Serious Situation 1

Since the calamity broke out on the night of the 28th of last month, Shanghai has been advancing towards an extremely dangerous and terrifying future. In these past few days, gunfire has wailed, flesh and blood have flown, and airplanes have bombed and fires have raged. Shanghai, the largest city in East Asia, and the three million innocent people residing in it, are now entirely trapped in the tragic state of destruction and terrorist attacks.

Since the inevitable disaster has now unfolded before us without hesitation, we can only resolutely shoulder it with heroic spirit and rise up to fight. Since the brutal Japanese have placed us in a dead end, finding a way to survive from this dead end lies in our own arduous struggle. Therefore, we should not be alarmed or terrified at the moment. We must, with a steadfast spirit, a calm attitude, unified steps, and planned and organized actions, rise up and fight the powerful enemy.

First, we believe that the business community should reopen immediately to stabilize people's hearts. Because a three-day strike is enough to express our unified, anti-Japanese, tragic, and intense emotions. However, if the strike continues for a long time, it will be enough to shake people's hearts, increase the terror in the rear, and reduce the strength of the general public's struggle, which is exactly what the Japanese are expecting and rejoicing in. For the sake of stabilizing order in the rear, it is necessary to reopen immediately and conduct business as usual.

Second, we believe that banks should also reopen immediately to stabilize finances. At this moment of widespread panic, if banks open but fear danger, they should request the concession authorities to be responsible for defense and maintenance. If there are fears that too many people will withdraw funds, causing turmoil, the banking industry can make its own arrangements to impose strict restrictions. Moreover, it is said that the banking industry is already well-prepared, so there is no need to worry. At the same time, society can impose righteous sanctions on this kind of action that disrupts finances and indirectly helps the enemy, without much concern. One day that the banking industry does not open, the social finance will not be stable for one day. And its impact will cause panic throughout the country. Therefore, the banking industry must open as soon as possible.

Third, we believe that all forces must be concentrated and carried out in various ways under planned and organized actions, such as comforting the soldiers at the front and providing relief to compatriots in the war zone. In addition to assisting local governments and concession authorities, a supreme authority should be established by various public organizations to comprehensively plan, concentrate forces, and jointly carry out relief activities. Comfort items must be suitable for the needs of soldiers at the front. Relief to disaster victims does not have to be confined to one place, and sound methods can be devised to send them to other ports. Other matters, such as adjusting food supplies, providing relief to the unemployed, maintaining transportation, and stabilizing order, should be jointly planned. Concentrating forces will yield greater results, while dispersing them will yield minimal results. We must clearly recognize this point.

In short, the Japanese are trying to create great terror in Shanghai. We must maintain social order and eliminate this terror with a calm attitude and planned and organized actions, so that the enemy's ambition cannot succeed.

A First in Concession History

Since the Japanese Marines blatantly disregarded everything on the 28th of last month and attempted to attack Zhabei in the Chinese territory, their advances and retreats have been based on the concessions to restrain our army's attacks. Since its establishment on November 17, 1843, the Public Concession has maintained its neutrality for eighty-nine years until this year, 1932. That is, during these eighty-nine years, in all international wars, whether between China and foreign countries, or between foreign countries, these foreign countries were important members related to the Public Concession. Each time, they were able to comply with the regulations and not exceed them regarding the neutrality of the Public Concession and the port of Shanghai. For example, during the Sino-French war in 1884-1885, the neutrality of the Shanghai port Public Concession was formally recognized first. After that, during the Sino-Japanese War between 1894 and 1895, the Sino-Russian War between 1904 and 1905, and the World War between 1914 and 1918, in all three international wars, no country related to the Shanghai Public Concession failed to recognize its neutrality and gave it absolute respect. However, this time, the Japanese army blatantly used the Public Concession as the starting point for its attack and as its base, repeatedly attacking the Chinese army. Its destruction of neutrality is a first in the history of the Public Concession.
