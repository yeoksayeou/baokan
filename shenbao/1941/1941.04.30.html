歐洲搏鬬的析視 10

巴爾幹戰爭結束了。這在整個戰局裏，並沒有最後的決定作用；在英國，並非最後的勝敗關頭；但是我們不能因此而忽略了這一戰爭的重要性。很明顯，巴爾幹戰爭一天繼續，德義就一天被迫在東西兩綫同時作戰；義大利的側翼，就一天在嚴重的威脅之中；英倫三島，地中海，大西洋，以及非洲等地的形勢，也就可以暫時和緩一下；所以英國在巴爾幹的失敗，影響於今後的戰略者，實甚重大。可是巴爾幹戰爭亦不夠判斷兩方力量的優劣。三星期的過程與結果，僅僅證明了兩件事：一是德義陸空聯軍在巴爾幹這一隅佔着優勢，一是英國目前還沒有集中力量，與德義即行決戰的意念和準備。兩方面在陸地與海洋上優劣形勢的對比，本是不可動搖的事實，至於在任何方面的較量，當然受此基本形勢的支配。德義去年可以在陸上擊潰西戰塲的英法聯軍，現在當然也不難擊敗央希南的聯軍於巴爾幹；可是進一步，如果德義尋不到陸上通路，而貿然擴大非洲戰爭，或不顧一切，現在就挑起地中海大戰，及向英倫三島侵襲，則他們的慘敗命運，當然也就早經注定，是不待說的。

在這樣的陸上與海洋對抗形勢之下，英國海軍現在幾乎已經完全控制了環繞歐非兩洲的一切海面，而還可供他加深控制的區域，祇有波羅的海，黑海，及亞得里亞海。另一方面，德義陸軍在歐洲大陸，現在也到了煊赫的最高峯，假如他不向蘇聯烏克蘭及土耳其衝進，則唯一可供牠發揮威力的對象，也就祇剩了伊伯里安半島上的西葡兩國。而且，即使在這些陸海區域內，現在及最近將來的任何可能發展和進退，也還是決定不了兩方面的成敗。所以，兩方面的陸海主力之間，即將出現一種僵持的局面，而不至立即舉行最後的決賽；可是空中戰鬭及軍備的競爭，當然要許多倍的較前加緊起來了。英德近來彼此的大空襲，以及英首相演辭中的强調美國空中機械接濟，那意義就在於此。

當然，僵持並不就是停頓。英德兩方一時雖然無法和對方決戰，但牠們當然要加速彼此陸上和海洋軍備的擴充，以準備那個大決戰來臨的期日；而同時，英國以牠海軍的壓倒優勢，當前所可從事的工作，大概不外於：（一）加緊對歐洲大陸的封鎖，企圖在重要原料及食糧短少的壓力下，使德義戰爭機構漸漸癱瘓，（二）確保本身的海上交通綫，使牠所有殖民地的物產資源，可以源源不絕地組織及納入牠的戰事機構，尤其重要的，是大西洋上對美交通安全的確保。至於德義方面，也勢必要盡量利用自已的一切優點，以進行：（一）以陸軍慢慢潛入非洲或近東，企圖由陸上逐漸瓦解英帝國，（二）趁當前的空軍及潛艇的些微優勢，拚命錘擊英國的海上航運，（三）用盡一切方法，破壞美國接濟的到達英倫三島。

總之，英國與德義兩方面，雖然都在一面加緊培養自已力量，一面尋覓對方弱點，以便作最後的一擊；但目前美蘇兩國的態度，如果長此不變的話，則在大淸算之前，世界全體就恐怕要注定了挨受長期的痛苦和掙扎。

岸英軍甫下增援時，所有的配備，將全不適用。不僅步兵如此，機械化部隊和輜重隊，亦因地域而受相當影響。在里比亞駛行的車輛，不適宜於崎嘔的阿比西尼亞，在這種塲所，只有利用駱駝或騾子遝輸的一法了。　　里比亞寒冷無比，氣溫常達在冰點以下。沙漠高原刮來的東南風，在夏天熾如爐火，在冬天却又冷如刺骨。　　在沙漠裏想要隨時舉槍開火，也極困難，來福槍和機關槍管，時常會被沙粒衆沒。阿剌伯人慣用破布裹住槍膛，塞子塞住槍口，防止沙粒。但行軍時倘遭遇敵方斥堠隊的埋伏，就要猝不及防，遭其毒手了　　此外，在沙漠的寒夜裏，除了古羅馬時代灰石挖成的水槽外，別無庇護之所。白天，大風揚起沙土，幾百輛車子踏着沙粒，冒着風暴前進，咫尺之內，不辨東西南北。彷彿置身在稠密的森林裏。走不到幾百碼，泥沙已塞沒了眼睛鼻孔和喉管，令人窒息欲死。　　沙漠戰爭且和陸地戰爭不同，祇有海戰差堪比擬。海上交鋒，兩軍在何處作戰，並無關係，即使通過國門，亦無大碍。祇要敵船遭受挫折，任它們前進多遠，絲毫不生影響。在沙漠裏作戰的唯一戰略，即在重創敵軍，使其人力物力遭受最重大的損失，而以消耗敵人，打倒敵人爲最後目的。　　關於沙漠戰爭所可說的，遠較在肥沃土地上的戰爭爲少。在沙漠作戰的兵士，必須忍受人類所可遭遇的一切痛苦，且祇有情緒上或美感上的慰藉　　沙漠戰爭，雖然困難重重，但也有引人入勝的地方。人類鬥爭史中最偉大的貢獻，多得之於曾受過沙漠中嚴酷鍛鍊的戰士。　　沙漠的美麗和沙漠的可憎都是無可比擬。記者曾視察過埃及西部的沙漠地帮，英國中東軍總司令華維爾將軍所部軍隊作戰的塲所，即在此沙漠，雖覺一片荒凉，而美麗的景色，亦有使人終身居此，其樂無窮之感。可是在數小時後重蒞其地。又覺牠的景色不過如瓦礫荒塲，黑暗的矮樹，猶如蹲伏待哺的許多石貓。　　上面的回憶，並非全是無爲而發，牠可以解釋爲什麽即使感覺最遲鈍的人，在沙漠中也要爲了所受的不安而抱怨，但却不願調往他處作戰，因爲沙漠戰爭都是憑着一股熱忱，這種戰鬥精神，有時爲他處戰爭所看不到的。當然有許多時候，沙漠戰爭不啻是厭倦和寂寞的一種安慰，但有時沙漠生活使人自感渺小，因而使天賦能力益覺敏銳。　　即使尼羅河英軍所橫貫的沙漠，够不上阿刺伯沙漠的偉大，可是牠所加於人類的種種磨難，却是不相上下。大熱，大冷，—雖則沙澳氣溫少有降到四十度以下的，但却常似在冰點下四十度光景—缺乏飲水，缺乏蔭蔽，以及烈日的蒸晒，暴雨的侵襲，對於不善攝生的人，確有莫大危險。　　沙漠中最利害的無過於風。當五句風挾着赤道一帶的沙土，從南吹來，空氣中便充滿了一股橙黃色的濃烟，使人目力所及，不能達十五呎之外。這時候祗有帶着面具，纔能呼吸不染塵土的空氣。　　風力非常强勁，能把軍用車吹離軌道，有如吹倒帳幕一般的容易。然而牠是偉大的和平使者，祇要大風一起，兩軍戰鬥就會立刻停止，此時人們的思想，除了避免窒息以外，其餘都顧不到了。　　這時氣溫驟升，陸上景物，逐一消滅，塵沙蔽空，不見天日，地中海的怒濤，像大砲一般打擊着山崖海岸，就是海戰也不得不被迫停頓了。　　五句風其來雖驟，共去亦速，一霎那間，遮天蔽日的黃烟，重回大地，於是交戰又開始了。　　到了冬天，沙漠的表面，有時被霜凍結，朔風怒號，順着乾涸的河道，吹過懸崖峭壁的絕頂，撲面生寒，猶如刀剌一般。這是沙漠中的兩個大患，人類聰明雖巳克服了無數困難，但是對這兩點，却至今無法應付。沙漠中英軍掘壕　　里比亞杜勃克附近戰况

Eldest Son of the US President Arrives in Chongqing - Paid Respects to Chairman Chiang Last Night - Will Fly to Chengdu Today for Sightseeing and then Proceed to Egypt - Purpose of Visit to China Reportedly for Military Study 1

Chongqing - James Roosevelt, eldest son of President Roosevelt, along with Major Thomas, arrived in Chongqing at 8 a.m. on the 29th via a China National Aviation Corporation (CNAC) flight from Hong Kong. Among those welcoming them at the airport were representatives such as Vice President Kung, Foreign Minister Wang, US Ambassador Johnson's representative, McHugh, Chinese and foreign journalists, totaling over a hundred people. James, quite tall and energetic, wearing a dark green suit and holding a raincoat, walked out of the airport with the reception staff. Photographers swarmed to take pictures, creating a busy scene. James, in a very welcoming tone, said, "President Roosevelt sends his regards to everyone." Then, Chinese and foreign journalists requested James to make a statement. He then met with reporters in the pilots' lounge, answering their questions in a very amiable manner.

(Question) Does the gentleman bring a letter from President Roosevelt to Chairman Chiang? Does Mrs. Roosevelt also have a letter for Mrs. Chiang?

(Answer) There are both letters and verbal messages. Mrs. Roosevelt does not have a letter for Mrs. Chiang this time, but before my departure, Mrs. Roosevelt had already sent a letter to Mrs. Chiang.

(Question) What is the mission of this visit to China?

(Answer) I am personally very interested in military affairs and transportation. I am here to study military aspects, but it is purely for learning and not, as rumored, on any advisory mission.

(Question) Before leaving the United States, did the gentleman meet with Dr. Currie to discuss the situation in the Far East?

(Answer) I met with Dr. Currie before leaving Washington, but I did not have the opportunity to read his report to the President on his visit to China.

(Question) Do American newspapers recently frequently publish news about China in prominent positions?

(Answer) Indeed, almost daily.

(Question) What is the general situation in the United States at present?

(Answer) Washington is very busy in general, with increased working hours for all kinds of jobs, and it is very intense every day. The strike situation has gradually subsided, and defense work is proceeding very smoothly.

(Question) What if you encounter Japanese aircraft on the way today?

(Answer) On the way, besides seeing beautiful clouds and a safe flight, there was nothing else to see.

An Analysis of the European Struggle 10

The Balkan war has ended. In the overall war situation, this does not have a final decisive effect; in Britain, it is not the final moment of victory or defeat; but we cannot therefore ignore the importance of this war. Obviously, as long as the Balkan war continues for one day, Germany and Italy will be forced to fight on two fronts, east and west; the flank of Italy will be under serious threat for one day; and the situation in the British Isles, the Mediterranean, the Atlantic, and Africa can be temporarily eased; so the failure of Britain in the Balkans has a very significant impact on future strategies. Berlin: The port of Valletta on Malta was heavily bombed by German aircraft yesterday. The dockyards suffered heavy damage, and the oil depot was hit and caught fire. The British Su Sanspu-class cruiser was hit and destroyed, and another small boat was hit and caught fire. (29th Haitong Dispatch)

British Warships Gather in Gibraltar; Conduct Defense Exercises 27

Vichy: According to news from Algiers, Gibraltar held a defense exercise on Sunday, including an attack by aircraft carried by an aircraft carrier. On that day, one battleship, one battlecruiser, one aircraft carrier, seven destroyers, and seven motor torpedo boats sailed into Gibraltar from the Atlantic Ocean. (29th Reuters Dispatch)

Anti-British Sentiment Strong in Spain; British Consulate Advises Citizens to Evacuate 28

London: It was heard here today from Berlin radio that the British consul in Spain, after 31 British people were arrested for anti-Spanish activities, has advised all British people to leave Spain. (29th United Press Dispatch)
